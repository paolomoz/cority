The Axion Health Product Legacy

Axion Health was a U.S.-based provider of cloud-based occupational health and employee wellness software, known for its ReadySet® platform.

Following the acquisition, Axion Health’s ReadySet® software was incorporated into Cority’s health software, enhancing Cority’s offerings in employee health, occupational health, and safety management.

Integrated Cority Health Software

Cority’s health solution is designed to manage occupational health, employee well-being, industrial hygiene, and ergonomics in a single, integrated platform.

Axion Health further strengthens Cority’s ability to deliver value to healthcare organizations by adding the best-in-class turnkey solution for occupational health.

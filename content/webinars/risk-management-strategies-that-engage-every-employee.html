Connecting Your Workers to Risk: Risk Management Strategies that Engage Every Employee

Risk management strategies is most successful when there's widespread employee buy-in. Learn how from our experts.

style
navy

The first step to keeping your employees healthy and safe at work is to identify what risks they are exposed to – but most companies only have a handful of employees on staff to manage risk. Instead of only a few dedicated EHS professionals managing risk for thousands of employees, companies should adopt new strategies that include every employee in the risk management process. As your first line of defense and best access to risk visibility, everyone from the shop floor to management should be empowered to easily identify and report risk across the organization.

Watch this webinar to learn how to:

  • Create a process and culture where employees are engaged to communicate their health risks
  • Implement different methods for your employees to identify and report risks
  • Empower employees with technology to actively participate in your risk management program

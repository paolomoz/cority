ASSP Safety Webinar 2025 : Keeping Your Eye on the Prize:  How to Refocus Your Safety Program to Ensure a Sustainable Safety Future 

In an ever-evolving work environment full of distractions – both internal and external – fundamental safety practices that form a foundation of a strong safety culture get lost in everyday operational battles. Organizations need to maintain focus on essential safety principles and their crucial role in preventing serious incidents. Gain a renewed understanding of how to integrate these best practices into daily operations and stay focussed on what really matters.

Whether you are a seasoned safety professional or new to the field, this session will equip you with the tools and mindset to build a safer workplace by reinforcing the fundamentals that ensure long-term success and well-being. Join Cority’s Farzhana Shah, P.Eng. to learn how organizations can foster a proactive, accountable, and positive safety culture by making lasting improvements through a back-to-basics approach.

What you will Learn 

  • Redefining how safety success is measured
  • The importance of engaging the front-line workers
  • Inspect, Inspect, Inspect
  • How to foster a learning environment that seeks to understand root causes rather than place blame
  • The role that technology can play in optimizing your safety performance

Forget Spreadsheets: Retail EHS Challenges Require EMIS Solutions

The role of Environmental Management Information Systems (EMIS) in developing a mature environmental, health, and safety compliance program.

style
navy

Environmental compliance is an increasingly complex topic for the retail industry. Nowadays, companies are required to adapt to limited staff and resources, general inspection concerns, and overwhelming data tracking spreadsheets.

Join Cority’s Amanda Smith, VP of Solutions Marketing and Enablement, and APTIM’s Steve Musser, Client Relationship Director, where they will have an in-depth discussion about these modern retail challenges and how to overcome them.

In this webinar, we will discuss:

  • The role of Environmental Management Information Systems (EMIS) in developing a mature environmental, health, and safety compliance program
  • Key considerations for a successful EMIS implementation and prolonged success
  • Examples of how EMIS can be utilized to monitor compliance programs such as waste, oil, and grease management

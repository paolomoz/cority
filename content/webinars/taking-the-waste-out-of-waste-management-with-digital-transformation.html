Taking the Waste out of Waste Management with Digital Transformation

Watch our webinar to learn how to better organize and execute your waste management plan to bring value and sustainability to your business.

style
navy

Waste management used to be mundane. You create waste, then properly store and dispose of it according to regulations that do not change often. There is now new excitement in the waste industry with the focus on Sustainability and Digital Transformation. Zero Waste initiatives and IIoT such as drones have kick started creative thinking. The result is the emergence of innovative ways to reuse waste as well as find money through operational efficiencies. Waste is becoming a key strategic program in most companies.

During this webinar, you will learn:

  • Common money pits in waste management
  • How to utilize technology to find efficiencies and cost savings
  • Strategies to elevate your waste program to bring value and improve sustainability

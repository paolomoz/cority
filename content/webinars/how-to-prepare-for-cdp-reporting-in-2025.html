With evolving requirements and growing expectations for transparency, preparing for CDP reporting in 2025 requires a strategic approach. This webinar will walk you through the latest updates, key reporting deadlines, and best practices to ensure a smooth disclosure process.

This webinar will cover:

  • The 2025 CDP reporting timeline and what’s changed
  • How CDP aligns with other sustainability frameworks
  • Best practices for organizing and preparing your data
  • Tips to enhance your previous years’ responses
  • How software can streamline and simplify your reporting

Whether you’re submitting for the first time or aiming to improve your score, this session will provide the guidance you need to optimize your CDP response. Register now to stay ahead of the reporting process!

Building a Culture of Accountability that Drives Safety Excellence

Learn how leaders can use EHS software, coaching, and metrics to build accountability and drive long-term safety excellence.

style
navy

Excellence isn’t an accident, it’s architecture.

In this session, Cority’s Sean Baldry shows how top-performing EHS+ programs are designed for consistency, turn data into decisions, and make accountability stick. You’ll see how standardized processes create copy-paste outcomes across sites, how to separate signal from noise so priorities are obvious and defensible, and how to engineer ownership into daily work with clear expectations, visible progress, and real follow-through.

We’ll cover what outperformers actually focus on to create durable advantage, the core elements of an accountability culture (plus the quiet pitfalls that undermine it), the actions that move the needle and how organizations are deploying software to reinforce these practices. If you want fewer heroic saves and more reliable wins, this session is for you.

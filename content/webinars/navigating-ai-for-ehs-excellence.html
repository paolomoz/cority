Artificial Intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing the landscape of Environmental Health and Safety (EHS) management, offering unprecedented opportunities for organizations to enhance their safety protocols and prevent workplace harm. Join us on April 18th at 2:00 PM ET for our “Fantastic Voyage: Navigating the Growing Opportunities of AI for EHS Excellence” webinar. Led by Cority experts Stuart Cook and Andrew Penner, this session will be your guide to understanding and leveraging AI in your EHS strategy.

  • The various types and branches of AI solutions and their value in EHS management.
  • How AI is transforming EHS practices, from data analysis to task automation and strategic decision-making.
  • Real-world examples of companies using AI to tackle EHS challenges and extract valuable insights from their data.
  • Practical steps for integrating AI into your EHS program and maximizing your return on investment.

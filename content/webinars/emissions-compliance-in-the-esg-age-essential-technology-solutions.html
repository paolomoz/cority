Emissions Compliance In The ESG Age: Essential Technology Solutions

Explore emissions compliance in the ESG age. Learn essential strategies for managing emissions and meeting regulations.

style
navy

Regulators, investors, consumers and publicly disclosed net zero targets are all piling on the pressure for organizations to reduce emissions. Consequently, investment in corporate emissions management and carbon reduction initiatives is ramping up – according to Verdantix research, more than half of firms will make double-digit increases in net zero and climate risk spend in 2023. Whilst carbon disclosures are often the focus of attention, EHS functions must not lose sight of their core mandate: to remain compliant with GHG and air emissions regulations and permits. As legislation evolves, the data requested of organizations grows more comprehensive, leaving firms with digitally immature infrastructure at risk.

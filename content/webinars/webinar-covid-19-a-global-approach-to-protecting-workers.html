Webinar: COVID-19: A Global Approach to Protecting Workers

Learn about the developments in health and safety legislation, the role of the European Union, its programs to improve the protection of workers, and give examples of legal developments to increase worker protection during the current COVID-19 pandemic in Germany and Switzerland.

style
navy

With the COVID-19 pandemic situation changing daily and as countries slowly begin coming out of lockdown, many people may be returning to the workplace. This webinar will discuss the current COVID-19 situation in Germany and Switzerland and will discuss the implications of the current epidemiology on the non-healthcare workforce. This webinar will also cover how to use various controls beyond PPE that can assist in stopping the spread of COVID19 in the workplace.

Join Roberta Smith, renowned occupational health leader and Director of Worker Health at Cority Axion Health, and Katharina Bockisch, EHS Regulatory Consultant and German lawyer, as they discuss developments in health and safety legislation, the role of the European Union, its programs to improve the protection of workers, and give examples of legal developments to increase worker protection during the current COVID-19 pandemic in Germany and Switzerland.

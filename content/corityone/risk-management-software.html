Risk Management Software Features

Guided Risk Assessments

Perform risk assessments via mobile app with out-of-the-box validated checklists for JSA, HAZOP, FMEA, What-If analysis, and more — or easily configure your own. Automated risk scoring and trend analysis built in to help you spend less time on calculations and more time on prevention.

Risk Scoring

Auto-calculate risk scores based on probability, severity, frequency, and other criteria. Get pre-built risk scoring formulas or customize your own.

Mobile App

Record hazards, report incidents, complete inspections and risk assessments, review existing records, complete tasks, update progress, and more. Anywhere in the field.

Increased Risk Visibility

Get advanced real-time insights and reports. Track key risk management inputs and outputs for tasks, hazards, control measures, and more. Monitor changes over time, prioritize remediation, assess response effectiveness, and benchmark performance.

Workflow Integrations

Sync risk data with other solutions across the Cority platform. Incident management, compliance, industrial hygiene, management of change, and more. Get complete visibility and control. Eliminate risk across locations and operations.

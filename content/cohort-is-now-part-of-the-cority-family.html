The Cohort Product Legacy

Cohort has a long legacy in UK occupational health software, rooted in NHS implementations from the 1990s. After expanding to broader public and commercial clients, it was acquired in 2012 by Medgate (now Cority).

Cohort’s solution has been integrated into Cority’s global health solutions, continuing as a leading occupational health software system.

Integrated Cority Health Software

Cority’s health software is a modular solution designed to manage occupational health, employee well-being, industrial hygiene, and ergonomics in a single, integrated platform.

Incorporating the strengths from Cohort, CorityOne now features a suite of comprehensive occupational health solutions.

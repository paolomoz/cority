Cority + Jincom

Jincom specializes in creating visual HSE content and training for organizations with diverse workforces worldwide. They transform complex, text-heavy content into simple, visual, engaging, and technically accurate visuals and animations. Their expertise includes developing Visual Standards, eBooks, e-learning courses, 3D animations, and Virtual Reality experiences.​

Jincom delivers this content through our platform, which integrates directly with Cority. Cority users can assign visual HSE content and training to specific users or user groups based on inspection, observation, or incident findings. This integration is seamless, requiring no additional applications or logins.​

Every month, their visually engaging content reaches over a million people, informing, educating, and training them on critical health and safety topics.​

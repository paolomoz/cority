Cority + Inseer

Inseer’s proprietary, AI-powered vision system enables organizations, with or without dedicated ergonomic experts, to capture ergonomic risk data quickly and effortlessly using a mobile device. The technology leverages Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and objective ergonomic assessments to measure risk factors to prevent musculoskeletal injuries.

Inseer is designed to prevent injuries before they happen using a rapid (less than 10 min), accurate (close to 98% accuracy), and non-intrusive video motion capture technology. Their technology is also easily adaptable to multiple different industries, including manufacturing, healthcare, utilities, and construction.

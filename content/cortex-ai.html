How does Cortex AI work?

Cortex AI offers a Control Center that connects multiple AI applications together across the CorityOne platform.

With Cortex AI, customers can orchestrate their AI workflows using out-of-the-box AI agents, or configure AI agents for their own use cases, even selecting the most appropriate AI model for each scenario.

Frequently asked questions

What makes Cortex AI different from other AI solutions?

Cortex AI is different in 4 key ways:

Cortex AI is built by EHS+ experts who understand how EHS programs operate and where AI can deliver the greatest impacts. This means that we apply AI where we know it matters most.

Cortex AI embeds AI into your workflows and daily tasks to help you see and address operating risks early. But we also know that we cannot design for every AI use case you may encounter. That’s why we’ve created the Cortex AI Control Center, giving you the power to curate AI in the ways you want, helping you adapt Cortex AI as your needs and EHS+ programs evolve.

With Cortex AI, we keep you in control. It is designed to give you full visibility and governance into what AI you’re using, what AI responses you’re getting, and how they are used. No other vendor offers this degree of AI control.

AI is only as powerful as the data that feeds and trains it. By helping you converge your EHS+ data within CorityOne, you have the most comprehensive and secure data set available to power your AI engine and deliver the insights that will truly transform your business.

What are some key use cases that Cortex AI supports?

Cortex AI supports a diverse set of use cases across all EHS+ domains, from speech-driven incident reporting and AI-powered medical transcription to invoice scanning to support carbon emissions reporting, and compliance permit analysis, enabling users to focus less on document reviews and more on tangible actions that drive performance improvement.

We don't want AI activated in our instance of Cority. Can we shut it off?

Yes. Cortex AI has been designed to be fully configurable based on the needs and wants of each organization.

Note that Cortex AI is set to ‘off’ by default. Customers can only activate Cortex AI after they sign a contract accepting Cority’s AI terms and conditions.

Once activated, customer administrators can toggle ‘off’ any of the Cortex AI Agents, whether just a few or all, at any time.

What if we only want specific use cases enabled in Cortex AI?

Like CorityOne’s overall system architecture, Cortex AI is designed to meet organizations where they are and grow with them.

The system has been designed to give organizations the ability to configure the tools and workflows they desire, with the ability to toggle ‘off’ the tools they don’t want. Cortex AI’s control center is designed to make activating or deactivating AI use cases quick and simple.

Through our AI Kick-Start projects, our team can help you determine what AI agents are best suited for you and ensure your pace of AI adoption matches your needs and goals.

Do customers need technical skills to use Cortex AI?

No technical skills are required to navigate, use, and find value from Cortex AI.

The Cortex AI Control Center is designed for EHS+ professionals and administrators, with no-code configuration and intuitive interfaces that keep users in control.

While Cority offers multiple deployment options to help customers get started with Cortex AI, we recommend that customers start with a AI Kick-Start Project.

With our AI Kick-Start Projects, Cority will deliver focused admin training, learning guides and resources, and curate a set number of AI use cases that will help customers get up and running fast.

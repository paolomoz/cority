Introduction

The purpose of this policy is to define Cority Software’s response to the Modern Slavery Act 2015 and constitutes our slavery and human trafficking statement.

Policy Statement

Modern slavery encompasses slavery, forced and compulsory labor, and human trafficking whereby individuals are deprived of their freedom and are exploited for commercial or personal gain.

Cority Software Inc. is committed to a zero-tolerance approach to modern slavery and to acting with integrity in all its dealings, relationships, and supply chains. It expects the same high standards from all its staff, suppliers, contractors, and those with whom it does business. Cority Software Inc. acknowledges the risk that a supply chain may involve the use of a hidden or unknown subcontractor reliant on forced labor.

Although Cority Software Inc., as a supplier of software, considers the risk of modern slavery to be low due to the nature of its supply chains, it takes its responsibilities to combat modern slavery seriously, as demonstrated by its promotion and adoption of the following policy measures:

  • The prevention, detection, and reporting of modern slavery in any part of its business or supply chains is the responsibility of all those working for Cority Software Inc. or under its control.

  • All supply chain lines need to be continually risk assessed and managed in relation to modern slavery and any high-risk suppliers audited.

  • Cority Software Inc. encourages anyone to raise any concerns about modern slavery and will support anyone who acts in good faith.

  • Cority Software Inc. will continue to develop its commitment to combat modern slavery and will provide staff training where appropriate.

Applicability

This policy applies to all employees, consultants, and other persons doing business with Cority Software Inc., including contractors and suppliers. The policy will be communicated to all employees and is available on the shared document environment. This policy will be made available to interested parties, subject to compliance with Cority Software Inc. classification restrictions.

Compliance

Any employee found to have violated this policy may be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

Approval

This document has been verified and formally signed off as correct and fit for purpose by the Cority Software Inc. Vice President of Human Resources.

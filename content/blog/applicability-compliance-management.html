Have you ever felt like you were drowning in EHS regulations? Or that you weren’t complying with all of the regulations you were supposed to? You’re not alone.

EHS professionals are tasked with managing so many state/provincial and federal regulations for their companies and must ensure compliance with each and every one of them. This gets even more complex if your company operates in multiple regulatory jurisdictions and is invested in different industry verticals. Knowing what rules are and aren’t applicable to your business is the first step to successful compliance management. But how do you figure out what’s applicable?   

Introducing Cority’s Applicability Module

To help make the regulatory compliance process easier, Cority has partnered with RegScan, a global leader in EHS regulatory content. EHS professionals can now leverage their subscription to RegScan directly in Cority to determine what regulations are applicable and then use this information to create tasks to ensure all facilities are meeting their compliance obligations. Additionally, clients will have access to RegScan’s auditing questionnaires which can be used to standardize and streamline the internal audit process. “We are excited to expand our partnership with Cority,” said Ned Ertel, President and CEO of RegScan. “The regulatory landscape continues to be ever-changing and being able to offer clients integrated solutions, like Applicability, to manage their compliance is imperative.”

Say Goodbye to Compliance Management Guesswork

RegScan’s regulatory content provides clients with an array of state/provincial and federal regulations and provides the context needed to determine how to comply with them. This eliminates the guesswork and ensures that all facilities are following the same process for identifying and managing compliance obligations. Plus, you’ll be able to quickly identify changes to regulations and what impact this will have on operations. As Mauri Paz, Cority’s Environmental Product Manager, noted, “Our collaboration with RegScan is another example of how we are leveraging partnerships to deliver a world class solution. It’s important for our customers to efficiently and effectively manage all compliance obligations in a comprehensive, centralized way to develop a best-in-class compliance program.”

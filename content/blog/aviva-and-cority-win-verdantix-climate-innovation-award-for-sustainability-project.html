Aviva and Cority Win Verdantix Climate Innovation Award for Sustainability Project

We are delighted to congratulate our customer, Aviva, for its recognition at the recent 2024 Verdantix Climate Innovation Awards. At the award ceremony held in London, UK on November 26, Aviva, the UK’s leading diversified insurer, was named winner of the ‘Climate Digital Champion Award’ alongside Cority for a joint transformative sustainability project. This accolade celebrates Aviva’s ambitious sustainability goals, achieved with Cority’s Sustainability Cloud solution, in response to evolving environmental, social, and governance (ESG) demands. 

In early 2023, Aviva, a leader in ESG with deep-rooted sustainability ambitions, selected Cority’s solution to unify and enhance its data management across global markets, drive accuracy, and streamline reporting on climate and sustainability metrics. This collaboration provided Aviva with a centralized, robust toolset, capable of automating data flows and optimizing emissions reporting and sustainability disclosures aligned with regulatory frameworks, including CDP, DJSI/CSA, and UNGC.

“We are thrilled to be recognized with Aviva for our work in supporting their goals to address climate change and embed sustainability across their business,” said Alex Hardwick, Director of Sustainability Planning & Enablement. “With Cority’s Sustainability Cloud, Aviva’s sustainability team has streamlined their processes, improved data accuracy, and gained valuable insights to drive climate impact reduction—all essential steps in their journey toward a more sustainable future.”

Project Highlights

Comprehensive Global Coverage

Cority’s Sustainability Cloud spans Aviva’s global markets, covering all relevant Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions.

Automation of Data Processes

Leveraging Cority’s centralized emissions factor library of over 1.5 million factors, Aviva automated the emissions calculations, saving time and ensuring data consistency.

Enhanced Framework Integration

Through Cority’s Frameworks module, Aviva streamlined sustainability disclosures, ensuring accurate and consistent reporting across all ESG frameworks and minimizing reporting duplication.

“Partnering with Cority has been instrumental in advancing our objectives for the collection, management and centralisation of our ESG data in support of our sustainability ambitions,” said Claudine Blamey, Group Sustainability Director at Aviva. “Their comprehensive solution has enabled us to add workflows across our ESG data collection processes, automate parts of it, and centralize this data to help meet a ‘capture once – report many times’ strategy. Holding ESG data and associated auditable evidence in this way is helping to provide the insight and reporting accuracy to drive meaningful climate action across our global operations.”

With Cority’s CorityOne Sustainability Cloud, Aviva could achieve significant efficiency gains in ESG reporting. This can minimize risks associated with ESG disclosures and enhance their ability to track and act on emission trends in line with global reduction targets. Cority’s Sustainability Cloud solutions can be leveraged standalone or as part of the comprehensive, software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform, CorityOne. The responsible business platform consists of a suite of connected solutions for managing environmental, health, safety, sustainability, and quality programs. Users can aggregate data from across the organization for a single, accurate, holistic view of EHS and sustainability performance.

This award underscores Cority’s dedication to providing innovative, scalable solutions that empower organizations like Aviva to meet their sustainability goals and navigate the complexities of ESG data and reporting with confidence.

Recognition marks the 7th and 8th Occupational Health & Safety wins for the global EHS software provider 

In a significant recognition of innovation and product excellence, Cority has been named a double winner of the Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) New Product of the Year Award for both its Permit to Work (PTW) solution and the Industrial Hygiene Calculation Engine. These prestigious awards highlight Cority’s commitment to advancing workplace safety and enhancing worker health through its state-of-the-art solutions, which closely align with EHS market trends and the priorities of global companies—such as integrated and consolidated solutions as well as digitization. 

“The push towards digitization in EHS&S (Environmental, Health, Safety, & Sustainability) is a much-needed technological advancement and a strategic necessity. Economic pressures and market dynamics are compelling companies to rethink their strategies. Digitization offers a solution to manage these pressures efficiently. It enables companies the ability to achieve cost savings, improve resource allocation, and ensure compliance cost-effectively.” – Bill Palmer, Product Marketing Manager, Environmental and Safety at Cority – EHS Trends Recap 

A Dual Win for Cority

Cority secured victories in two distinct categories, further solidifying its status as a leader in the industry:

Category #1: Worker Health

2024 – OH&S Winner for Product: Industrial Hygiene Calculation Engine

Cority’s Industrial Hygiene Calculation Engine stood out for its ability to help organizations proactively manage worker health. By providing accurate exposure assessments, risk analysis, and regulatory compliance tools, this product ensures that employees are protected from hazardous conditions.

The innovative solution expands Cority’s market-leading SaaS Industrial Hygiene (IH) suite, available within CorityOne, the company’s integrated EHS SaaS-based platform.  Cority’s IH Calculation Engine provides a scalable and seamless path for future growth by providing professionals with a powerful, flexible tool to design advanced hygiene exposure equations, streamline standard workflows, and alert leaders to emerging risks.  

The Industrial Hygiene Calculation Engine offers a significant advancement in managing workplace exposures by enabling IH professionals to create complex equations that accurately monitor exposures to agents such as radiation, noise, and temperature extremes. It facilitates rapid comparison of calculated results against permissible limits, ensuring compliance and guiding data-driven decisions for workplace safety. 

Moreover, leaders and decision-makers can effortlessly visualize calculation results on reports and advanced dashboards to guide real-time EHS strategy and optimize decision-making on prioritization and resource use. Cority is the only EHS enterprise software provider offering such advanced IH calculation functionality.

Category #2: Workforce Management Systems

2024 – OH&S Winner for Product: Permit to Work

Cority’s Permit to Work (PTW) solution also earned top honors in the Workforce Management Systems category. This software solution streamlines the permit management process by automating workflows, minimizing administrative burdens, and ensuring that all safety protocols are met before any work begins. The tool enhances efficiency while maintaining the highest standards of safety and compliance. 

Cority’s PTW solution goes beyond just managing permits—it transforms how organizations approach high-risk tasks. With features like mobile-enabled reporting, configurable workflows, and advanced analytics, it offers a comprehensive toolkit for driving safety, compliance, and performance improvements across the entire workforce.

Managing high-risk tasks in the workplace can be a daunting challenge for many safety leaders. When the stakes are high, there is no room for error; the smallest oversight can result in significant harm to workers or even threaten the future of a business. Cority’s PTW solution empowers businesses to confidently navigate these high-risk scenarios, ensuring safety, compliance, and operational excellence.

Powerful Functionality Tailored to Your Company 

Cority’s PTW solution offers a range of advanced features designed to meet the diverse needs of organizations:

  • Mobile-Enabled Tools & Templates: Easily request work permits using customizable templates directly from a mobile device, streamlining the process and ensuring timely approvals, even from the field. 
  • Configurable Workflows: Tailor approval workflows for different permit types, and visualize permits on a real-time map to identify potential conflicts and avoid dangerous overlaps. 
  • Deep Integrations that Drive Value: Seamlessly link permits to contractors, enhancing project oversight and ensuring all personnel have the required training and permissions. 
  • Advanced Business Intelligence & Analytics: Track key performance metrics to assess the effectiveness of your PTW program and leverage these insights to make real-time decisions that improve safety and efficiency. 

Protecting Workers & Fostering a Culture of Safety  

As OHS noted in their announcement, “As the landscape of workplace safety continues to evolve, safety companies are stepping up to the challenge, delivering cutting-edge solutions to meet the dynamic needs of industries across the globe. The role of these innovations cannot be overstated, especially as companies prioritize keeping their workers safe amid emerging risks and compliance requirements.” 

Cority’s dual-category win is a testament to the company’s dedication to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in workplace safety. Its solutions help organizations not only meet compliance requirements but also foster a culture of safety that protects workers in every aspect of their daily duties.

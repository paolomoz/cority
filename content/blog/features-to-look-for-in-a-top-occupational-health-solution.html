Top Features of Occupational Health Solutions

1. Streamlined Appointment & Clinic Management

Efficient clinics are the backbone of occupational health, and Cority’s solution takes clinic management to the next level. From appointment scheduling to health record management, Cority simplifies every step of the process:

  • Appointment management with intuitive scheduling and reminders
  • Comprehensive clinical testing, including pulmonary function testing (PFT), audiometric, and vision testing
  • Integration with testing devices for seamless, real-time data capture
  • Secure electronic health records (EHR) to consolidate employee health data
  • ePrescriptions to streamline medication management
  • Immunization tracking and automated medical surveillance workflows
  • Management of body fluid exposure cases, diagnostic imaging, and telehealth services

2. Advanced Case Management for Comprehensive Oversight

Cority’s case management features help organizations monitor, document, and resolve occupational health incidents efficiently:

  • Manage multiple case types (injury, illness, leave) in a single platform
  • Track absences, work restrictions, and modified duties
  • Use to-do lists that aggregate next steps for every case
  • Utilize integrated disability guidelines to support timely recovery
  • Automated regulatory forms and reports for compliance

Learn more in our blog: Occupational Health, Case Management, and Disability Duration Guidelines.

3. Robust Surveillance Programs

Cority automates medical surveillance to help identify and mitigate risks:

  • Customizable protocols for audiometric and vision testing
  • Automated alerts to ensure timely testing
  • Secure medical record storage for quick access
  • Real-time immunization campaign tracking

4. Comprehensive Drug Testing & Travel Health Management

Cority supports complex drug and travel health needs with:

  • Integrated drug testing programs
  • Extensive drug database for compliance
  • Travel management tools for immunization and testing requirements

5. Mobile Solutions for a Global Workforce

Cority’s mobile tools support EHS operations anywhere:

  • Largest mobile feature set in occupational health
  • Multi-language capabilities
  • Offline functionality for remote locations
  • Browser-based access, no app updates needed

6. Real-Time Learning & Training Compliance

Cority’s LMS supports ongoing health and safety education:

  • Automated training enrollment
  • Extensive content libraries and customizable paths
  • Mobile-friendly training options
  • Self-service learning and compliance tracking

The Voices of Cority: Internal Subject Matter Experts

At the heart of Cority’s ecosystem of leading EHS & sustainability software solutions is a team of in-house experts, each bringing a wealth of hands-on experience and specialized knowledge in fields ranging from regulatory compliance to occupational health to safety and risk management to sustainable supply chain procurement.

Cority’s growing team of subject matter experts is comprised of former safety managers, engineers, nursing professionals, mining experts, and other industry veterans who have a deep, first-hand understanding of the daily challenges EHS leaders face. Many of these experts keep their industry certifications up-to-date and participate in both voluntary and mandatory continuing education opportunities to keep their credentials up to date. Their unique perspectives (and up-to-date industry know-how) enable Cority to develop intuitive, user-centered solutions tailored to address the real pain points of practicing EHS and Sustainability professionals.

This focus on user-centered product development and design has played a pivotal role in Cority’s journey from a software provider to an industry leader. With each feature built, every update released, and every new solution launched, Cority’s internal experts ensure that our software solutions remain at the forefront of meeting evolving industry needs. As a result, this dedication to excellence has been recognized through numerous industry awards and accolades. 

In fact, Cority’s 250+ internal industry experts have a combined (astounding) total of over 5.4k (estimated) years of experience. Across fields including Aerospace, Automotive, Chemical, Construction, Healthcare, Mining, Oil & Gas, Pharmacy, and Government.

For each solution cloud, there is between 18-51 internal team members with an accredited background in Health, Environmental, Safety, Sustainability, Quality, or Analytics, all contributing to the future of the platform.

Learning Through Collaboration: A Culture of Continuous Improvement

Cority involves SMEs and partners in the feedback and product roadmap planning process alongside customers and advisory boards in order to ensure existing products continue to meet market demands and new ones are aligned with future needs.  

These processes include:

CABs – Customer Advisory Boards meet approximately three times a year to talk directly with admins and users about upcoming product releases, product challenges and enhancements they believe should be top priority and to facilitate discussion between customers to uncover industry-specific hot topics and needs.  

Customer Request Board – Cority’s product teams of subject matter experts manage and monitor a customer request board, where any user can submit a request for a new feature/enhancement, etc. Product-focused experts then directly communicate with customers to better understand their needs and translate those into product requirements for consideration. 

User Testing – As an integral part of Cority’s commitment to usability, all major features and enhancements undergo thorough user testing. This process involves subject matter experts (SMEs) and end-users who provide valuable insights and feedback. This direct input helps ensure that Cority’s solutions meet the real-world needs of both frontline users and administrators, directly influencing the continuous improvement of our products. 

Cority Connect – Cority Connect is our annual event that provides a unique opportunity for direct engagement with our customers. This face-to-face interaction allows us to gather feedback, discuss enhancements, and better align our solutions with customer needs. It’s also a platform to share best practices and preview upcoming releases, ensuring our offerings are at the forefront of industry demands and innovation. 

Discovery Projects – Each year, Cority sets out on several “Discovery Projects” that are inspired by leading industry frameworks, including Pragmatic Marketing. These projects are collaborative efforts involving our product teams and external experts and are designed to explore new areas that could impact our product roadmap. This proactive approach helps us stay ahead of market trends and ensures that our solutions continually evolve to meet emerging customer needs. 

Cross-Departmental Communication and Voice of the Customer (VOC) Interactions –At Cority, we break down silos between Customer Success, Product Teams, Professional Services, and other departments to ensure a seamless flow of communication. When customers raise concerns or suggestions, these cross-functional interactions enable us to swiftly address their needs. This collaborative environment not only enhances product development but also ensures customer feedback is promptly and effectively integrated into our service improvement strategies.

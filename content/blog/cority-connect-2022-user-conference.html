In the not-too-distant future, EHS and Sustainability leaders and professionals from all over the world will be arriving in San Diego, CA to Reconnect with one another at Cority’s annual user conference, Cority Connect. Cority Connect is one of the leading and largest Environmental Health & Safety user conferences in the industry and offers a unique networking opportunity for clients to interact with fellow users, industry experts, and the Cority Team. It’s been two years since the last live, in-person conference, so here’s a little information on what to expect.

The Details

Cority Connect will take place in San Diego, CA on June 6-9, 2022 at the InterContinental Hotel and will include 3 days of insightful presentations, hands-on training, networking, customer stories, and a compelling keynote presentation. The goal of this year’s conference is to Reconnect with one another, share industry knowledge, and showcase advancements in the CorityOne platform. New and existing users will be able to interact face-to-face and gain a deeper understanding of how the CorityOne platform enhances their workplace safety, sustainability, worker well-being, and operational excellence.

Keynote Speaker: Dan Thurmon

Our Keynote Speaker, Dan Thurmon will present on Tuesday with an insightful presentation surrounding topics of embracing uncertainty, empowering change, and the future of employee engagement & work-life balance. As a dynamic keynote speaker, author, and performer, he has delivered thousands of presentations worldwide for corporations, associations, and educational institutions. We’re thrilled to have Dan Thurmon at Cority Connect and to gain more knowledge on using uncertainty, change, and chaos to learn, lead, and transform – both personally and professionally.

Lions and Tigers and Bears, Oh My!

Every year there’s an official offsite dinner party and we’re excited to announce that this year’s official event will take place on Wednesday evening at the San Diego Zoo – and it’s sure to be a roaring good time! You’ll be able to reconnect with one another, check out the animals, and enjoy dinner and drinks under the San Diego sun before heading home the following day.

Reconnect With Us

There’s so much to learn at Cority Connect – whether you’ve attended in the past or this is your very first time, you’re guaranteed to leave feeling more knowledgeable and empowered. Cority Connect is an amazing opportunity to hear from industry leaders, Cority experts, and customers who utilize the CorityOne platform to fit the needs of their business and ensure success. You won’t want to miss out on this unique experience, so register now and secure your tickets today before sales close on May 25th. We hope to see you there!

Visit corityconnect.com for more information on the agenda, accommodations, FAQs, and COVID safety.

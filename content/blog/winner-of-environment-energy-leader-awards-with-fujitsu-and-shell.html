Cority wins two Environment + Energy Leader Awards for Fujitsu and Shell partnerships.

In an era where environmental sustainability is a global imperative, Cority stands out as a beacon of innovation and dedication. We are thrilled to announce that Cority has been honored with two distinguished Environment + Energy Leader Awards. Our transformative partnerships with two multinational, household-name companies, are a testament to what visionary alignment on sustainability goals can achieve.  

A Journey of Collaboration and Sustainability with Shell

“Shell’s collaboration with Cority demonstrates a proactive approach to waste management and sustainability, leveraging innovative technologies and overcoming implementation challenges to drive operational efficiency and achieve environmental objectives.” – E+E Leaders Award Judge  

“Shell’s collaboration with Cority demonstrates a proactive approach to waste management and sustainability, leveraging innovative technologies and overcoming implementation challenges to drive operational efficiency and achieve environmental objectives.” – E+E Leaders Award Judge  

Cority’s Waste Management Solution helps organizations manage waste in accordance with applicable laws. It quickly demonstrates compliance to regulators and tracks waste storage locations and limits, types of wastes on-site, and disposal costs. Organizations can then use this information to make short and long-term operational decisions. 

Fostering Environmental Accountability with Fujitsu

“Many companies (including my own) are struggling with measuring Scope 3. Engaging suppliers methodically and productively is a key challenge. This solution with Fujitsu is a very good approach that appears to have been successful and instituted a continuous improvement process.” – E+E Leaders Award Judge  

“Many companies (including my own) are struggling with measuring Scope 3. Engaging suppliers methodically and productively is a key challenge. Since 2008, Cority and Fujitsu have forged a dynamic partnership, with Cority’s solutions at the heart of Fujitsu’s sustainability initiatives. With Cority, they can meticulously calculate, monitor, and report GHG emissions, encompassing Scopes 1, 2, and 3, across nearly 180 European sites. This granular data management has helped them set and achieve ambitious environmental targets and ensure transparency for key stakeholders.  

A Future Forged in Sustainability

These awards from are more than accolades; they reflect Cority’s unwavering commitment to driving sustainable change through innovation and partnership. As we celebrate these milestones with Shell and Fujitsu, we are reminded of the power of collaboration in tackling global environmental challenges.

Cority is not just facilitating compliance and reporting. We are enabling a movement towards a more sustainable and responsible business ethos. As we look to the future, we are excited about the possibilities that lie ahead in our journey towards environmental excellence. Join us as we pave the way toward a sustainable future, one innovative solution at a time.

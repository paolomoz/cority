Compliance & GMP

Ensure adherence to GMP, FDA, EMA, and EHS regulations through controlled workflows, audit trails, change management, and document control—supporting quality operations and regulatory readiness.

Safety & Incident Managment

Capture safety incidents, near misses, and non-conformances using mobile or desktop tools. Trigger investigations, root cause analysis, and CAPA (corrective & preventive action) to protect staff, patients, and products.

Occupational Health

Track exposures, clinic visits, vaccinations, and health surveillance. Automate workflows, follow-ups, and return-to-work plans to safeguard employee health in regulated environments.

Industrial Hygiene

Monitor airborne, noise, chemical, and lab exposures. Use mobile-enabled sampling and analytics to ensure compliance with exposure limits and support a healthy workforce.

Quality & CAPA Management

Drive quality with integrated CAPA, audits, document control, and non-conformance workflows. Enable traceability and compliance with ISO 9001 and GMP standards.

Environmental Compliance

Manage emissions, waste, and permits including hazardous waste and water discharge. Automate compliance reporting and inspections to support sustainable operations.

Analytics & Reporting

Access dashboards showing safety incidents, CAPA effectiveness, exposure trends, quality metrics, and environmental KPIs—empowering data-driven risk reduction and continuous improvement.

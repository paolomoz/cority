EHS Software for Aerospace & Defense: Core Capabilities

Deliver end-to-end oversight for complex aerospace and defense operations - supporting secure facilities, supply chains, and multi-national certification programs. Embedded AI available to surface risks faster.

Compliance & Certification

Manage regulatory compliance, supplier audits, and industry-specific certifications (such as AS9100). Cority ensures documentation, workflows, and training align with aerospace and defense standards to reduce audit risks.

Environmental & Emissions

Track air emissions, wastewater, and hazardous waste across manufacturing and maintenance sites. Automate environmental reporting to meet EPA, EASA, and other global regulations—reducing manual effort and ensuring accuracy.

Safety & Incident Management

Capture safety incidents, near misses, and non-conformances with mobile-ready tools. Drive investigations, root-cause analysis, and corrective actions—while reporting to OSHA and international bodies with ease.

Chemical & Material Controls

Maintain inventories of chemicals, composites, and regulated materials. Use SDS and REACH workflows plus container tracking to support manufacturing and maintenance compliance in controlled environments.

Risk & Process Safety

Automate risk assessments and management of change for aerospace &/or defense manufacturing and maintenance processes. Apply critical controls and link assessments to corrective actions to help predict and adjust processes to minimize or remove disruption

Training & Competency

Configure dashboards for user safety metrics, waste tracking, and product compliance status for manufactured goods. Gain actionable insights via trend analysis, benchmarking across facilities, and custom executive reporting.

Analytics & Reporting

Assign and track safety and compliance training for employees. Automate recertification reminders, validate competencies, and document certifications required for AS9100, ITAR, and export control.

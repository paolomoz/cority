Oil & Gas EHS+ Solutions

CorityOne enables real-time visibility, streamlined workflows, and advanced analytics across the EHS+ spectrum to drive safer, more sustainable oil and gas operations.

Capture safety incidents, near misses, and hazards from field to refinery. Use Cority’s mobile and desktop tools to streamline investigations, root-cause analysis, and corrective actions—raising situational awareness and reducing risk.

Track air emissions, wastewater, spills, and waste across sites. Automate regulatory reporting for the EPA, Toxic Release Inventory (TRI), and permit systems—driving cleaner, audit-ready operations.

Manage the full lifecycle of chemicals and waste—from storage and transfers to spill response and disposal—with container-level tracking and SDS integration for enhanced safety and compliance.

Record clinic visits, exposure monitoring, respirator use, and fatigue tracking. Automate health workflows and tailor follow-up plans to protect field and plant personnel.

Standardize safety, environmental, and operational audits. Track inspection outcomes, corrective actions, and follow-ups to ensure performance consistency and regulatory adherence.

Digitize high-risk task permits with approvals, isolation checks, and real-time verification to ensure safe execution during maintenance, construction, and shutdown activities.

Leverage dashboards and KPI analytics on safety, environment, health, and quality metrics. Support enterprise benchmarking, risk prediction, and informed decision-making across operations.

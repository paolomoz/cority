Retail EHS+ Solutions

Enable consistent management of safety, health, environmental, and compliance across store networks by leveraging real-time data, mobile tools, and standardized workflows for improved operational control.

Safety & Incident Management

Capture safety incidents, near misses, and observations at store or distribution center level using mobile or desktop tools. Automate investigations, root-cause analysis, and CAPA workflows to reduce harm and liability.

Retail Compliance Audits

Deploy standardized compliance checklists and store audits. Track completion status, corrective actions, and performance trends to ensure regulatory and corporate policy adherence.

Occupational Health

Monitor employee clinic visits, exposures, ergonomic risks, and wellness programs. Use configurable health workflows and follow-ups to support staff well-being and reduce absenteeism.

Environmental Management

Track waste streams, recycling, and environmental impacts at the store level. Automate inspections and reporting to support sustainability programs and regulatory compliance.

Chemical & Spill Response

Manage cleaning agents and store chemicals by tracking SDS, container inventories, and spills. Equip staff with mobile workflows to respond, document, and report incidents effectively.

Permit & Task Control

Configure permits and task management workflows for store maintenance and contractor work. Ensure proper authorizations, safety checks, and audit trails are in place across all retail locations.

Analytics & Reporting

Access dashboards monitoring safety, health, compliance, and environmental metrics. Benchmark performance across stores, identify trends, and support data-driven decision-making.

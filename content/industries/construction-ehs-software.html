Construction EHS+ Solutions

Centralize environmental, safety and quality processes in a single EHS+ platform, arming workers and subcontractors with the data needed to control operating risks, maintain compliance, and drive performance.

Mobile Solutions

Workers and subcontractors can record incidents, task completions and compliance checks performed in the field using offline-capable mobile tools, eliminating secondary admin effort, enhancing data accuracy, and delivering real-time insights across job sites.

Permit‑to‑Work Management

Empower the safe and compliant execution of high-risk tasks, from planning through verification, via configurable, mobile-first digital forms, approval workflows and offline capabilities that adapt to dynamic jobsite conditions.

Contractor Management

Easily register, prequalify, train, supervise, and evaluate contractors and subcontractors across multiple projects and job sites, helping you hold these firms accountable to EHS+ performance standards just like your own workforce.

Environmental Management

Oversee all environmental emissions related to constructions projects in a centralized location. Confidently monitor compliance with automated workflows and standard reporting tools to help make quick decisions when warranted.

Industrial Hygiene

Capture and analyze dust, noise, and heat exposure data across worksites to assess risks early, trigger alerts and contingencies, and take steps to protect worker health before it results in injuries and illnesses.

Quality Management

Integrate quality workflows - audits, document control, SPC, non-conformances, and CAPA - with EHS+ processes to support ISO 9001 and drive continuous improvement across all facilities.

Business Intelligence

Unlock real-time visibility into safety, health, hygiene, and environmental KPIs with powerful and configurable BI tools and data visualization options. Benchmark performance, uncover trends, and drive smarter decisions to drive performance.

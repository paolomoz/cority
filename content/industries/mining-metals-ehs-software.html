Mining & Metals EHS+

Centralize operational safety, environmental stewardship, workforce wellbeing, and quality management practices in a single, unified EHS+ platform tailored specifically for mining operations, smelters, and processing sites.

Incident Management

Easily capture and analyze hazards, near misses and safety incidents on desktop or mobile, even when offline. Automate investigation workflow, optimize root cause analyses, and create impactful corrective actions that prevent future events.

Occupational Health

Streamline comprehensive health management for staff working in remote and harsh environments. Document clinical encounters, optimize health surveillance and easily manage return-to-work plans in one application.

Air & Water Management

Track environmental emissions and discharges across sites with automated sampling, configurable workflows and purpose-built reporting tools to drive compliance and ensure transparency.

Chemical & Waste Control

Manage hazardous substances from import through processing, storage, and ultimate disposal. Track containers, safety data sheets, spills, and waste flows—improving safety, compliance, and audit readiness.

Contractor Management

Easily register, prequalify, train, supervise and evaluate contractors and subcontractors across multiple projects and job sites, helping you hold these firms accountable to EHS+ performance standards just like your own workforce.

Industrial Hygiene

Capture and analyze dust, noise, radiation and heat exposure data across worksites to assess risks early, trigger alerts and contingencies, and take steps to protect worker health before it results in injuries and illnesses.

Sustainability Management

Easily capture, consolidate and validate EHS+ data, routing it through standardized reporting templates aligned to key frameworks, while enabling full traceability of data back to its sources.

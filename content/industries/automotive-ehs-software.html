Automotive Solutions

Strengthen compliance and organizational wellbeing across the plant floor with a unified EHS+ platform - supporting ergonomics, environmental programs, inspections, and real-time analytics.

Cority supports automotive teams with comprehensive occupational health tools, including medical surveillance, case management, clinic operations, and return-to-work workflows - helping protect worker health and streamline care delivery.

Capture hygiene monitoring data and analyze exposure risks with built-in calculation engines - supporting mobile data collection, real-time alerts, and compliance with standards like OSHA and NIOSH.

Capture and assess ergonomic risks using AI-driven motion capture technology and standardized scoring methods. Support proactive interventions with mobile video uploads, automated risk scoring, corrective action tracking, and integration with broader EHS data.

Enable real-time capture of safety incidents, near misses, and non-conformances on any device. Automate investigations, root-cause analysis, CAPA assignments, and timely follow-up to prevent recurrence.

Manage air emissions, wastewater, waste tracking, and environmental permits in one platform. Automate regulatory reporting, inspections, and compliance checks to reduce manual effort and support sustainability goals and audit readiness.

Drive Quality 4.0 transformation with integrated quality tools - document control, audits, SPC, PPAP, FMEA, supplier quality, and CAPA workflows - supporting IATF 16949 and ISO 9001 compliance.

Digitize permit-to-work workflows across plant-floor and maintenance operations. Ensure reliable approvals, authorizations, and contractor safety with secure, standardized processes at scale.

Amplify actionable insights via dashboards that monitor ergonomics, health, safety, environmental, and quality metrics. Benchmark facilities, assess operational safety trends, and drive continuous improvement.

Chemical Industry EHS Management Capabilities

Manage chemical industry EHS requirements with comprehensive software for chemical manufacturing: centralize risk, compliance, and safety in a single EHS+ platform.

Risk & Hazard Management

CorityOne automates risk assessments, hazard analyses, and corrective actions, ensuring compliance with OSHA PSM/RMP, EU Seveso, and other global standards. Capture and control hazards before they escalate at every facility.

Process Safety Controls

Implement permit-to-work, change control, and management of change modules tied to process safety. Enforce critical controls, work authorizations, and traceability to reduce incidents and support audits.

Chemical Inventory & SDS

Maintain up-to-date chemical inventories and ensure that published safety data sheets are current. Automate hazard communication, GHS labeling, and container tracking to support operational safety and regulatory compliance.

Incident & Non‑Conformance

Capture incidents, near misses, non‑conformances, and observations easily using your mobile device or desktop. Initiate investigations, identify root causes, and implement corrective and preventive actions, which are all connected to compliance and trending dashboards.

Compliance & Reporting

Streamline regulatory reporting across jurisdictions. Cority consolidates data to produce submission-ready reports for RMP, TRI, UK CLP, and other frameworks—while tracking audit readiness.

Training & Competency

Assign, track, and document safety training, certifications, and competency evaluations. Automate refresher reminders, verify qualification compliance, and protect workers and operations.

Analytics & Dashboarding

Configure KPI dashboards, loss trend analytics, and site benchmarking. Identify leading and lagging indicators, visualize performance, and drive continuous improvement across chemical facilities.

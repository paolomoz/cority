Time to Roll Up Your Sleeves: How to Run an Effective Workforce COVID-19 Vaccination Campaign

Learn best practices and recommendations on occupational health, safety, infectious disease, and risk management to help promote workforce COVID-19 vaccinations.

style
navy

As the world enters Year 2 of the COVID-19 pandemic, organizations are facing a whole new series of challenges.

OSHA guidelines recommend employers offer vaccines to employees free of charge (if possible) and also provide employee training on the benefits and safety of the vaccines developed to battle the virus.

But what else can and should you do to protect your workers from the persistent thread of COVID-19? How can your organization increase workplace vaccination rates and manage vaccination data accurately and efficiently?

This guide provides a collection of industry best practices and recommendations from our team of occupational health, safety, infectious disease, and risk management experts to help you increase the rate of workforce COVID-19 vaccinations. Plus, it covers how to improve overall vaccination management to reduce the risk to your people and your business.

[INFOGRAPHIC] Serious Injuries and Fatalities (SIFs) in the Workplace: What SIFs Are and How to Prevent Them

This infographic defines and explains what SIFs are and how to reduce life-altering or fatal work-related injuries and illnesses.

A serious injury or fatality is a work-related injury or illness that is life-threatening, life-altering or fatal.

In its 2022 survey, Verdantix noted – for the first time – that ‘SIF prevention’ topped the list of key priorities for corporations.

  • 6 in 10 firms surveyed identified ‘SIF prevention’ as the critical focal point for their EHS strategy.
  • In 2023, 50% of firms indicated the same.

Preventing serious injuries and fatalities (SIFs) has become the issue that EHS professionals and executives alike are talking about. This infographic explores the ins and outs of SIFs and techniques to prevent them using digital solutions.

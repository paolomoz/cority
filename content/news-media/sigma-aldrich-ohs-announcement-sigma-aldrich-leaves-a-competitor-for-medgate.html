Sigma-Aldrich Leaves a Competitor for Medgate

style
navy
  • Last Updated: March 25, 2026

Sigma-Aldrich, a leading life science and high technology company, becomes the latest company to switch from a competitor’s occupational health software to Medgate. Medgate is pleased to announce that Sigma-Aldrich plans to use the complete Medgate software solution – Occupational Health, Case Management, Safety, and Industrial Hygiene – for its Global OHS operations.

Sigma’s chemical and biochemical products and kits are used in scientific research, including genomic and proteomic research, biotechnology, pharmaceutical development, the diagnosis of disease and as key components in pharmaceutical, diagnostic and other high technology manufacturing. Sigma-Aldrich operates in 40 countries and has 7,600 employees providing excellent service worldwide. Sigma-Aldrich is committed to Accelerating Customers’ Success through Innovation and Leadership in Life Science, High Technology and Service.

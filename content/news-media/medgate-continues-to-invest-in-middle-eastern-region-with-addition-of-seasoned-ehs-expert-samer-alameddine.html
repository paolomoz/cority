Medgate Continues to Invest in Middle Eastern Region with Addition of Seasoned EHS Expert, Samer Alameddine

navy

EHS software leader continues its expansion in Europe, Middle East and Africa with key wins, increased services, and a growing team of experts

Toronto, ON (February 10, 2017) – Medgate, the global leader in enterprise-grade, SaaS-based Environmental, Health, and Safety (EHS) risk management solutions, has appointed Samer Alameddine as Senior Solutions Consultant for the Middle East.

Mr. Alameddine is a recognized expert in the EHS industry. He holds a MSc in Environmental Technology from the prestigious Imperial College London and a BSc in Environmental Health from the American University of Beirut. He brings a wealth of EHS knowledge and experience to Medgate, having worked as an Environmental Consultant for market-leader ERM and as an Industrial Hygienist with Golder Associates.

Alameddine joins a growing roster of Medgate professionals, a broad team that is by far the largest group of EHS industry domain experts in the industry. He brings with him an extensive understanding of client needs and goals, and the unique market demands in the Middle East. He is the latest member to join Medgate’s growing EMEA team, a rapidly expanding group poised to become the clear market leader.

“We’re very excited to welcome Samer to the team. In the past year Medgate has rapidly expanded into the Middle East, and Samer will be key to our success going forward,” said Medgate Senior Director of International Sales, Mike Jackson. “Samer’s vast experience is an asset to our Middle East team, and brings great value to our many customers in this region.”

“I’m excited join the ranks of Medgate’s subject matter experts. It’s rare to have the opportunity to work alongside EHS experts who are involved in shaping an EHS product. Because we truly know the everyday struggles of EHS professionals, we’re in the best position to add the most value to our customers, and the industry as a whole,” said Alameddine. “On top of this, Medgate’s product is constantly evolving, adding new – and improving existing – functionality.  I’m looking forward to building on Medgate’s unprecedented growth.”

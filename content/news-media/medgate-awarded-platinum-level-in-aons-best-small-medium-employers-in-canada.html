Medgate Awarded Platinum Level in Aon’s Best Small & Medium Employers in Canada

style
navy
  • Last Updated: July 8, 2026

Medgate is honored to have been named a Platinum Level winner of Aon’s Best Small & Medium Employers in Canada. This annual study, conducted by AON Hewitt and Queen’s University, identifies 50 companies that lead the pack in engaging employees, demonstrating effective leadership, building a strong corporate culture and leveraging the strength of their employer brand.

The Aon Best Employers Study is the only national workplace research of its kind, providing an in-depth view into employee opinions, comprehensive external benchmarking, and the expertise to identify the key priorities that will drive engagement and performance in your organization.

Aon’s methodology reflects over 20 years of learnings and best practices from Best Employer and employee engagement studies conducted all over the world.

On Aon’s announcement of the award recipients, Medgate President Mark Wallace said, “Medgate has been fortunate to win awards for business performance, but this award matters more than the others to me. It is based on an anonymous survey of the views of our employees. Engaged, motivated employees are a key to strong business performance and to a winning corporate culture. I am honored that we have been recognized as a Platinum Level employer.

“At Medgate, we pride ourselves on equipping our employees with the tools they need to get the job done. The success of this company is tied to the mutual respect between management and employees. Medgate offers best-in-class benefits, meaningful work, flexible work hours, competitive pay, and an enjoyable work environment, all while remaining transparent and minimizing politics and bureaucracy.

“We have a fantastic team working at Medgate, and they are the ones behind this success.”

Medgate Ranks Among the Fastest Growing Companies in Canada

style
navy
  • Last Updated: March 23, 2026

Medgate has been named one of Canada’s Fastest Growing Companies by Canadian Business and PROFIT. Medgate appears at No. 268 on the 28th annual PROFIT 500 which ranks Canadian businesses by their five-year revenue growth.

Medgate made the 2016 PROFIT 500 list with five-year revenue growth of 204%.

“Growing the company is important,” said President Mark Wallace. “but what we have been able to achieve – sustained, profitable growth every quarter for over a decade – is remarkable. This has put us in a position to invest in our product to become the leading EHS Software provider globally. The success of the past five years has earned us the ranking on this list, but for Medgate the next five years will be even more impressive.”

“Companies become a part of the PROFIT 500 through innovative thinking, smart strategy and sheer grit,” says James Cowan, Editor-in-chief of PROFIT and Canadian Business. “These firms demonstrate what Canadian entrepreneurs can achieve, both at home and across the globe.”

Medgate has been won several awards over the past year, including recognition as one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies, one of Canada’s Top Small & Medium Employers for 2016, one of the Best Employers in Canada; being shortlisted for the Canadian Chamber of Commerce’s Private Business Growth Award; and winning Techconnex’s Tech Company of the Year.

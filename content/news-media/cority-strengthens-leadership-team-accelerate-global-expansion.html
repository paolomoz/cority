Cority Strengthens Leadership Team to Accelerate Global Expansion

Leading EHSQ SaaS provider announces appointment of Pablo Neiman as Vice President, Professional Services and promotion of Greg Durand to Senior Vice President, Global Sales

Toronto, Canada – Feb 28, 2018 – Cority, the most trusted environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) software for assuring client success, today announced it has appointed Pablo Neiman as Vice President, Professional Services and promoted Greg Durand to Senior Vice President, Global Sales to accelerate the company’s global expansion.

“Since joining Cority, Greg Durand has significantly grown our high-performance sales team and more than doubled sales. Pablo Neiman’s track record of delivering large-scale enterprise implementations and driving operational efficiency will be invaluable as we enter our next stage of growth. Together, these enhancements to our leadership team will help us further our global presence and continue to help our clients achieve their health, safety, and sustainability goals,” said Mark Wallace, President and CEO, Cority.

Mr. Neiman replaces the retiring Jill Brown and brings over 10 years of experience leading high-performing teams and delivering complex software implementations for top-tier enterprises to his new role. Prior to joining Cority, Neiman served as VP, Strategic Planning and Operations at NexJ Systems, and rapidly rose through the ranks during his tenure at the organization – having previously held the positions of Director, Professional Services and Manager, Professional Services. Neiman received his M.A.Sc. in Engineering and Executive MBA from the University of Toronto.

Mr. Durand joined Cority in 2014 as Vice President, Global Sales and brings over 20 years of experience in sales, business development, and marketing management experience in the technology, finance, and medical sectors to his role at Cority. With a strong track record of growing organizations in mature and upstart markets, Greg has led operational improvements and revenue growth in his previous positions at leading firms like PriceMetrix, Algorithmics (now IBM) and Johnson & Johnson. Mr. Durand received his B.Sc. in Physics from the University of British Columbia.

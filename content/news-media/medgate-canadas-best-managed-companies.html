Medgate Named One of Canada’s Best Managed Companies

style
navy
  • Last Updated: March 9, 2016

Medgate was named one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies in 2015 for excellence in business performance. The Best Managed program recognizes Canadian-owned and managed companies with revenues over $10 million demonstrating strategy, capability and commitment to achieve sustainable growth.

“CIBC congratulates Medgate on being named one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies – a reflection of its strong leadership, sound business planning and focus on growth,” said Jon Hountalas, Executive Vice-President, Business and Corporate Banking, CIBC.

Medgate has received this award thanks to their management team’s commitment to profitable growth, increasing revenues by over 25% year on year for five years while nurturing a best-in-class company culture that supports employee development. The Deloitte judges were particularly impressed by Medgate’s commitment to customer service excellence.

Established in 1993, Canada’s Best Managed Companies is one of the country’s leading business awards programs recognizing Canadian-owned and managed companies that have implemented world-class business practices and created value in innovative ways. Applicants are evaluated by an independent judging panel on overall business performance, including leadership, strategy, core competencies, cross-functional collaboration throughout organization, and talent.

“Medgate’s vision is to be the most trusted brand, globally, in EHS software. Medgate has maintained sustained, profitable growth. Companies do not become successful overnight. This award is a testament to the hard work of all our employees over many years.”

“It’s a great privilege to win Canada’s Best Managed Company Award. We’re proof that Canadian companies can compete with the best in the world and that we can attract the best talent in the marketplace,” said Mark Wallace, President of Medgate.

“Best Managed companies are strong in strategy, capability, commitment and financial performance. They are a great Canadian business success story,” said Peter Brown, Partner, Deloitte and Co-Leader, Canada’s Best Managed Companies program.

The Best Managed program is sponsored by Deloitte, CIBC, National Post, Smith School of Business and MacKay CEO Forums.

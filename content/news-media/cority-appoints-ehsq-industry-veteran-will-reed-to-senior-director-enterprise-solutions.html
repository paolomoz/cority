Cority™ Appoints EHSQ Industry Veteran Will Reed to Senior Director, Enterprise Solutions

style

Cority customers to benefit from extensive industry expertise that will enable Sustainability, Operational Excellence and Worker Wellbeing initiatives.

Toronto, Canada – September 25, 2018 – Cority is pleased to announce and welcome Will Reed as Senior Director, Enterprise Solutions. Reed brings a wealth of knowledge and over 20 years’ experience in the Environmental, Health, Safety and Quality (EHSQ) industry, and is widely recognized as a trusted advisor, speaker and expert.

Most recently, he was Partner at ERM, a leading global provider of environmental, health, safety, risk, and social consulting services. Reed has a proven record of delivering exceptionally high value solutions to global Fortune 500 organizations.

Reed’s mandate at Cority will be to help new and existing customers drive value from their EHSQ initiatives. He will collaborate with Cority’s strategic partners and be responsible for the oversight of complex opportunities that require a highly consultative approach to ensure alignment of their business needs with Cority’s capabilities.

“We are thrilled to have Will Reed head up this strategic role at Cority,” says Greg Durand, Senior Vice President, Global Sales at Cority. “His deep industry knowledge, combined with the breadth and depth of the Cority EHSQ SaaS product will allow us to provide dynamic solutions that drive additional value and better business outcomes for our clients globally. As a firm that is leading the way in providing innovative solutions and superior delivery, this role will add to the Cority stable of industry experts and further position Cority as a trusted advisor for EHSQ Executive teams.”

“Cority has created EHSQ software solutions for industry that are incredibly powerful and differentiated,” says Reed. “By expanding the legacy portfolio significantly to include deep environmental, sustainability, and quality capabilities, Cority provides customers compelling business insights in a complete true SaaS offering. I am extremely excited to be joining the Cority team.”

Cority Reveals Speaker and Sponsor Lineup for Cority Connect 2019

style
navy
  • Last Updated: January 10, 2019

Premier industry event brings together global community of environmental, health, safety, and quality professionals

Toronto, Canada – January 10, 2019 – Cority, the most trusted environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) software for assuring client success, today announced the speaker and sponsor lineup for Cority Connect 2019, its 19th annual user group conference.

Cority Connect is one of the largest EHSQ engagement, training and knowledge sharing events in the industry and is being held in New Orleans from February 26 – March 1, 2019.

With over 40 sessions across five dynamic subject areas, attendees will have the opportunity to participate in hands-on training, earn continuing education credits, network with peers, learn best practices from Cority’s partners, and hear how industry experts and Cority clients are advancing sustainability, safety culture, worker well-being, and operational excellence initiatives in their organizations.

Confirmed sponsors include Title sponsor Arcadis, Platinum sponsor ERM, Gold sponsor RegScan, Silver sponsors Golder, Frostbyte, and SiteHawk, Bronze sponsor Verisk 3E, and Exhibitor sponsor Amplivox.

“Arcadis could not be more delighted to serve as the title sponsor for Cority Connect 2019,” said Connie Prostko-Bell, Associate Vice President, Arcadis. “We are eager to collaborate with the rest of the Cority community to share ideas and innovations that elevate EHSQ thinking, promote EHSQ performance, and deliver value to stakeholders.”

Keynote presentations include:

  • Nigel Packham, Associate Director, Safety and Mission Assurance, NASA Johnson Space Center

Packham will share insights and methodologies that can be used across land, sea, air, and outer space to successfully mitigate and manage risk.

  • David Metcalfe, CEO, Verdantix

Metcalfe will discuss emerging technology trends that are reshaping EHS processes and share new research findings from over 400 senior EHS professionals.

Featured speakers from the following companies will share lessons learned and outline their EHSQ program successes:

  • Airbus
  • Consolidated Edison
  • Dow Chemical Company
  • Los Alamos National Laboratory
  • Marathon Petroleum
  • Pfizer
  • Raytheon
  • Sobey’s
  • Tennessee Valley Authority
  • Toyota Motor Manufacturing Canada
  • Vopak

Other Cority Connect 2019 highlights include:

Partner Pavilion Industry experts and thought leaders from Cority’s implementation, content, and technical partner ecosystem will be present to share best practices for integrating technology and optimizing EHSQ programs.

Accelerator Workshops Interactive, hands-on training workshops will provide both super users and new users with in-depth, practical knowledge to leverage the Cority platform to continuously improve program performance.

EHSQ Excellence Awards Dinner Attendees are invited to network and celebrate the exceptional EHSQ achievements of their peers.

“For 19 years Cority Connect has been the hub for the EHSQ community to come together to collaborate, share best practices, and strengthen relationships,” said Mark Wallace, President and CEO, Cority. “This year’s attendees can expect compelling keynotes, valuable insights from Cority clients, and gain the technical training and actionable takeaways needed to take their EHSQ programs to the next level.”

To view the agenda and register for Cority Connect, visit www.corityconnect.com

Cority Recognized for COVID-19 Solution in 2020 ISHN Readers’ Choice Awards

Annual awards program recognizes the most innovative occupational health and safety products of 2020

Toronto, ON – July 31, 2020 – Cority, the most trusted provider of environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) solutions, today announced it received the 2020 ISHN Readers’ Choice Award for EHS Management Software for its COVID-19 Return to Work Solution. With this solution, occupational health and EHS practitioners can effectively manage the impact of COVID-19 on an organization’s people and processes while supporting a reliable and efficient business recovery effort.

Now in its 8th year, the ISHN Readers’ Choice Awards recognize the industry’s best occupational health and safety products, with thousands of ISHN subscribers and industry professionals voting for their favorite safety equipment suppliers and technologies. This marks Cority’s third consecutive year winning an ISHN Readers’ Choice Award, being recognized last year for its Predictive Analytics and Process Hazard Analysis solutions.

With Cority’s COVID-19 Return to Work Solution, occupational health and EHS practitioners can effectively mitigate pandemic and business continuity risks facing their organization. The software offers advanced data collection and analysis tools, enabling organizations to manage every area of their pandemic planning and response efforts while ensuring the health and safety of employees, maintaining essential business operations, avoiding unnecessary downtime, and minimizing costs.

Practitioners also have access to an interactive dashboard that provides a real-time, 360-degree view of an organization’s pandemic response. This allows program managers to quickly access their data to understand what’s working and where more support is required.

“Since COVID-19 hit, managing business and trying to define the ‘next normal’ has been a challenge for organizations across the board,” said Amanda Smith, VP of Solutions Marketing and Enablement, Cority. “With the support of Cority’s COVID-19 Return to Work Solution, our clients can build a dynamic pandemic response and business recovery effort while keeping their employees safe.”

This recognition comes on the heels of the announcement that Unilever has deployed Cority’s Occupational Health and COVID-19 solutions to aid the company’s pandemic response and streamline employee health management.  

To learn more about how to effectively manage the impact of the pandemic and influenza on your employees, visit our COVID-19 Solution page.

Cority Earns Top Product of the Year Award from Environment + Energy Leader for Second Consecutive Year

Annual awards program recognizes products based on their success in providing significant sustainability and energy management results

Toronto, ON – July 21, 2020 – Cority, the most trusted provider of environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) management solutions, today announced that its Air Emissions Site Diagram solution received a Top Product of the Year Award in the elite Environment + Energy Leader Awards program. The program’s panel of independent expert judges considers Cority’s solution a top example of the exemplary work in the fields of energy and environmental management.

Now in its 8th year, the Environment + Energy Leader Awards is a program recognizing excellence in products and services that provide companies with energy and environmental benefits. This is the second consecutive year Cority has received a Top Product of the Year award, previously winning for its industry-leading Environmental Management solution.

“With rapid advancements and a near-constant rate of change in the field, sustainability and energy professionals had to prove to our judges that they were really the best of the best this year,” said Sarah Roberts, Environment + Energy Leader publisher.

Cority’s Site Diagram is part of the company’s Air Emissions solution, which provides environmental professionals with a visual representation of their end-to-end air emission management processes. This enables practitioners to view complex asset-to-asset relationships, streamline emissions management programs, track permit compliance, and identify opportunities to reduce emissions, all from a single, unified solution.

One judge said of Cority’s Air Emissions Site Diagram solution, “This is a great tool. Most of these EHS systems use a database structure and it gets complicated when you go to set up a large site. By using a graphical interface tied to the database it makes setting this up a lot more intuitive. Overall, this product would be very useful and would allow other people, like facilities engineers, to assist the environmental side in setting up an emissions database.”

“It’s an honor to be recognized for environmental management product excellence,” said Michael Couture, Chief Product Officer, Cority. “By providing our clients with the ability to configure and manage their emissions inventory from a single point of reference, they can uncover new opportunities to reduce their emissions footprints, support corporate sustainability goals related to the reduction of hazardous air pollutants and greenhouse gases, and drive continuous improvement.”

To learn more about Cority’s Air Emissions Site Diagram solution, visit our emissions management software page.

Cority Ranked Top Five EHS Software Brands Worldwide by Independent Research Firm

Global survey assesses EHS Leaders’ preferences of the 34 most prominent EHS software vendors

Toronto, Canada – November 21, 2018 – Cority, the most trusted provider of Environmental, Health, Safety, and Quality (EHSQ) software for assuring client success, today announced it has placed in the top five preferred EHS software brands ranking by independent research firm Verdantix.

The 2018 Global EHS Leaders Survey: EHS Software Brands looks at the responses of 411 senior EHS decision-makers across 35 countries and 25 industries to understand software brand preference and awareness in firms with high to very high EHS risk profiles. Brand preference is defined as respondents perceiving an EHS software vendor as having market-leading or strong capabilities.

The report noted, “these results demonstrate that Cority’s recent rebrand from Medgate in June 2017 – a move driven by its acquisition of regAction and IQS – has been very successful.”

“Prior Verdantix survey data indicated that customers have a desire to buy a single, integrated EHS software platform,” said Yaowen Ma, Senior Analyst at Verdantix. “Cority has responded by expanding their environmental compliance, quality and chemical management solutions and their successful execution is reflected in this years’ survey results.”

“It’s an honor to rank as a top five recognized EHS software provider globally,” said Mark Wallace, President and CEO, Cority. “This acknowledgment from the Verdantix Global EHS Leaders Survey is a testament to what continues to fuel our rapid global expansion – a true SaaS product, our team of EHSQ experts, robust partner ecosystem, and industry-leading solutions that ensure our clients can improve worker-well being, advance sustainability initiatives, and achieve operational excellence.”

The announcement comes on the heels of Cority’s recent recognition for product innovation, having received two Occupational Health and Safety New Product of the Year awards for its predictive analytics and ergonomics management solutions, and being named a market leader in the 2017 Verdantix Green Quadrant benchmark of EHS software applications.

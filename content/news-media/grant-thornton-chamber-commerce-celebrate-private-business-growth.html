The Canadian Chamber of Commerce and Grant Thornton celebrate private business growth

style
navy
  • Last Updated: November 30, 2015

Medgate is honored to have been named among the ten finalists in the Private Business Growth Awards 2015. The award recognizes the integral role Canada’s private businesses play in our country’s economy by celebrating the success of those that have implemented strategic growth. The ten Canadian private businesses were selected for their commitment to such sustainable, holistic growth strategies.

The ten finalists were celebrated at a gala dinner at the Design Exchange in Toronto on November 25, where Global Relay was announced as the winner.

Medgate President Mark Wallace said: “Medgate’s key to success is that we have an extraordinary corporate culture, based on core values of product innovation, customer satisfaction, and respect for employees. My thanks to Grant Thornton and the Chamber of Commerce for celebrating the success of private companies in this country.”

“Growing a successful, sustainable private business is no easy feat—but it can be done,” said Phil Noble, Executive Partner and CEO, Grant Thornton. “The purpose of the Private Business Growth Award is to showcase those companies that are getting it right—fearlessly introducing new product lines and entering new markets, developing new employee engagement strategies and demonstrating different models of leadership—and setting their businesses up for long-term success. We think this is an important thing to celebrate and it’s an important message to relay to others.”

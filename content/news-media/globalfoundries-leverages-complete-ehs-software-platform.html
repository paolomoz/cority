GLOBALFOUNDRIES leverages complete EHS software platform

style

  • Last Updated: May 5, 2016

Semiconductor manufacturer GLOBALFOUNDRIES has gone live with Medgate’s integrated EHS software platform at their facility in New York state.

By choosing Medgate as its software partner for safety, occupational health, industrial hygiene and environmental information management, GLOBALFOUNDRIES can now coordinate EHS activities across their organization on a single platform. They have moved from their existing spreadsheet and database systems to realize the benefits of an integrated application that addresses their data management needs. They can now easily create combined reports that provide a wider view of the health and safety of their employees.

About GLOBALFOUNDRIES

Launched in March 2009, GLOBALFOUNDRIES has quickly achieved scale as one of the largest foundries in the world, providing a unique combination of advanced technology and manufacturing to more than 250 customers. With operations in Singapore, Germany and the United States, GLOBALFOUNDRIES is the only foundry that offers the flexibility and security of manufacturing centers spanning three continents.

About Medgate

Medgate leverages its cutting-edge SaaS-based technology and broad EHS product suite to partner with organizations around the world to effect meaningful change in the way environmental, health, and safety is managed. Medgate stands by its customers as they attain performance goals, reduce EHS risks, enhance corporate productivity, and create a healthier workforce.

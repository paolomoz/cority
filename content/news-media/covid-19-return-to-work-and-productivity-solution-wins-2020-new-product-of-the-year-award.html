Cority’s COVID-19 Return to Work and Productivity Solution Wins 2020 New Product of the Year Award from Occupational Health & Safety

Cority recognized for health and safety technology innovation by panel of independent judges

Toronto, ON – October 2, 2020 – Cority today announced its COVID-19 Return to Work and Productivity Solution has won a 2020 New Product of the Year Award from Occupational Health & Safety in the EHS Software category. This is the second industry award Cority has received for its COVID-19 solution this year, previously winning a 2020 ISHN Readers’ Choice Award.

Now in its 12th year, the New Product of the Year award program honors the outstanding product development achievements of health and safety manufacturers whose products are considered noteworthy in their ability to improve workplace safety. This year’s program garnered entries in 26 award categories, with an independent panel of industry judges choosing the winners.

“This year, safety products and services are being recognized on a global scale, bringing greater awareness to the important work completed by manufacturers in the industry,” said Sydny Shepard, Editor, OH&S. “Now more than ever, it is of the utmost importance to keep workers and employees safe from emerging and existing workplace hazards.”

Cority’s turnkey COVID-19 Return to Work and Productivity SaaS solution enables organizations to rapidly operationalize their pandemic response plans, systematically monitor and report on the health status of employees, contractors, and visitors, easily complete contact tracing, and streamline a safe and efficient return to operations. Purpose-built with industry best practice workflows, health surveillance tools, and intuitive visual dashboards, Cority provides occupational health and EHS practitioners with real-time access to critical indicators and actionable insights needed to manage a dynamic pandemic response and return to work program with speed and confidence.

“There were a number of great products submitted for the New Product of the Year Awards,” commented judge Greg Zigulis, president of Sixth Sense Safety Solutions. “Each year the products and their features just seem to get better. There were also some interesting products that were entirely new conceptually and responsive to the COVID-19 pandemic.”

“It’s an honor to be recognized by OH&S for our COVID-19 solution,” said Amanda Smith, VP Solutions Marketing and Enablement, Cority. “Using our software, clients can easily identify exposure trends and infection hot spots, monitor the status of employees who are off or in quarantine, and support their prompt return to work and productivity. This is particularly critical as we enter a potential second wave.”

Over 200 global organizations are currently using Cority’s market-leading health and safety software to navigate the COVID-19 pandemic and return to productive business operations.

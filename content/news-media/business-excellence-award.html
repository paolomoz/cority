Medgate Wins Canadian Business Excellence Award for Private Business

style
navy
  • Last Updated: July 6, 2026

Medgate is delighted to have been named a winner of the Canadian Business Excellence Awards for Private Business.

Since 1984, the Canada Awards for Excellence have been presented to private and public sector organizations of all sizes that have proven they are world class. This award is based on the Canada Awards for Excellence Standards and Requirements, which are used by numerous organizations as a management model for continual improvement and to achieve better operational results. This prestigious award is tangible evidence of an organization’s level of excellence. The Patron of the Canada Awards for Excellence program is His Excellency, the Right Honourable David Johnston, C.C., C.M.M., C.O.M., C.D., Governor General of Canada.

Medgate President Mark Wallace said, “The key to Medgate’s success lies in our corporate culture, which is based on our core values of product innovation, customer satisfaction, and respect for employees.

“We’re thrilled to have won this award. My thanks to Excellence Canada and PwC, along with the awards committee and all other sponsors.”

The Canadian Business Excellence Awards Committee includes Canada Wide Media, CFIB, Carleton University, CBC, CCAB, CEO Global Network, Excellence Canada, MaRS, and PwC.

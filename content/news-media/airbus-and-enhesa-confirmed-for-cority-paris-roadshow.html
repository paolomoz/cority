Airbus and Enhesa confirmed for Cority Paris Roadshow

style
navy
  • Last Updated: May 6, 2026

Birmingham, UK – April 29, 2019 – Cority, the most trusted environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) software for assuring client success, is pleased to announce it will be hosting its European Roadshow in Paris on 20 May 2019, at the Hotel Napoleon.

A day to learn, to meet EHSQ experts and to benefit from business opportunities in a unique place. Airbus’ Environmental Management System and ISO 14001 Certification Manager, Cindy Kloehn will be sharing with delegates how integration and good data management can support the effectiveness of environmental management systems. Enhesa’s Head of Thought Leadership, Global EHS Regulatory Compliance, Tjeerd Hendel-Blackford will also deliver a global regulatory and policy update on (mental) health and wellbeing and the current laws and policies governing health and wellbeing in countries around the world.

Attendees will then receive a live demonstration of the Cority platform and are welcomed to join the team for a cocktail reception.

Find out more and register for the free event.

Following Cority’s Paris Roadshow, the company will be exhibiting at Europe’s leading health and safety Preventica expo, 21-23 May, where it will showcase its industry-leading EHSQ SaaS platform at stand L56. Designed by certified industry experts, Cority’s solution empowers organisations to centralise, streamline and standardise critical EHS and Quality information and risk management across the enterprise.

To see Cority’s environmental, health and safety software solution in action, stop by stand L56 or visit www.cority.com to schedule a personalised demo.

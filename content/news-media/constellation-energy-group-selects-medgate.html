Constellation Energy Group Selects Medgate

style
navy
  • Last Updated: April 11, 2011

Medgate is delighted to announce that Constellation Energy Group (CEG) has selected Medgate as their solution provider for occupational health, safety, and industrial hygiene software, after completing a RFP and selection process. They will be using Medgate’s Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) software suite for enterprise-wide OHS data management.

Constellation Energy has a long and pioneering history in the energy industry. From its roots as the nation’s first gas light utility, the company has evolved into one of the largest and most innovative energy companies in America. Constellation Energy is a publicly traded (NYSE ticker: CEG) Fortune 500 leader (#149 on the 2010 list), headquartered in Baltimore, with nearly 10,000 employees, approximately 12,000 megawatts of generation capacity and $14.3 billion in annual revenues (2010).

AllOne Health expands use of Medgate

style
navy
  • Last Updated: April 8, 2016

Medical third party service provider AllOne Health has implemented Case Management and Training as part of their ongoing use of Medgate’s Occupational Health systems. This deepens their partnership with Medgate and allows AllOne to streamline their case management and training processes to support their Absence Management team in their injury triage services and training tracking.

“We’re delighted that AllOne Health has decided to extend their partnership with us and we look forward to continuing to help them improve employee health,” said Medgate’s VP Global Sales, Greg Durand, “This expansion is a reflection of the great relationship we have with AllOne and speaks to the comprehensive nature of our Occupational Health suite.”

AllOne Health works with global organizations by coordinating medical and behavioral services while helping managing escalating healthcare costs. They provide nearly 1,000 organizations with occupational health services and has access to a national network of over 5,000 clinics, 70,000 clinicians, IT Professionals and consultants.

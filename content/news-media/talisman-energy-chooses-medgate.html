Talisman Energy Inc. has completed its implementation of Medgate’s Occupational Health suite.

Talisman is a global oil and gas exploration and production company headquartered in Calgary, Alberta. It is one of Canada’s largest independent oil and gas companies, and was founded in 1992 by the renaming of BP Canada Ltd.

Talisman has revenues of over $7.7bn and is listed on the Toronto and New York stock exchanges under the symbol TLM.

Cority Recognized for Environmental Management Excellence with Multiple New Product of the Year Award Wins

EHSQ SaaS Provider’s Waste Management and Applicability Assessment solutions recognized by panel of industry experts 

Toronto, ON – April 26, 2018 – Cority, the most trusted environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) software for assuring client success, today announced it has won Environmental Protection’s 2018 New Product of the Year Award in the Environmental Management and Waste categories for its Applicability Assessment and Waste Management solutions.

Now in its 9th year, the New Product of the Year Award honors the outstanding achievements of industry manufacturers whose products make environmental professionals’ jobs easier. The 2018 contest attracted a record number of entries and Cority emerged as the only software provider to win two awards.

“This was the second year we conducted this contest in the spring, and environmental companies responded strongly with high-quality, innovative new products,” said Jerry Laws, Editor of Environmental Protection. “Health and Safety/Cleanup, Software/SaaS, and New Technology—Industry were very competitive categories this year, and our judges gave their highest scores to winning products in the New Technology—Industry category and also the Waste category. Our contest shows that environmental equipment manufacturers continue to do an outstanding job of meeting their customers’ needs in this fast-moving marketplace.”

Cority’s Applicability Assessment solution provides EHSQ professionals with the comprehensive set of tools needed to easily and accurately identify applicable regulations across their operations, leveraging compliance content from Cority’s partners. EHSQ professionals can build their own applicability questionnaires or maximize subscriptions with leading regulatory content providers directly in Cority. Cority’s solution was recognized for its:

Evergreen Compliance Management – ensures EHSQ professionals are notified of new regulations and regulatory changes so they can take action to maintain compliance.

Enhanced Auditing – standardizes and streamlines EHSQ auditing programs with out-of-the-box questionnaires based on applicability assessment results.

“Cority’s Applicability module was clearly designed with the client in mind,” said Ned Ertel, President and CEO, RegScan. “Their solution makes it easy for clients to leverage their relationship with RegScan but get the most from a single system to manage regulatory compliance requirements and auditing content.”

Cority’s Waste Management solution provides EHSQ teams with a single, enterprise-wide platform to manage hazardous waste generation and onsite storage Cority’s Waste Management solution earned the highest score out of all nominated Software/SaaS products. Environmental Protection’s judges recognized the solution for its configurable waste codes that ensure the proper identification of waste streams and its ability to generate appropriate labels and manifests. “Cority does everything that companies need to efficiently manage their waste streams and remain compliant,” said Fred Elliott, a judge on Environmental Protection’s New Product of the Year .

“We’re proud to be recognized by Environmental Protection for our best-in-class environmental compliance and waste management solutions,” said John Easton, Director of Product Management, Cority. “We continue to lead the market because our solution is designed by EHSQ professionals who have been in the shoes of our customers and understand that proactive environmental management should be simple.”

Vifor Pharma Selects Medgate’s Workplace Safety Software

style
navy
  • Last Updated: December 30, 2012

Medgate is pleased to announce that Vifor Pharma has selected our GX2 safety software suite to manage workplace safety data for their Swiss-based operations.

Vifor Pharma, a fully integrated speciality pharmaceutical company of the Galenica Group, researches, develops, manufactures and markets pharmaceutical products worldwide. The company focuses on the treatment of iron deficiency and offers a diversified portfolio of prescription medicines as well as over-the-counter (OTC) products. Vifor Pharma operates in four franchises: Iron, Vifor Fresenius Medical Care, Renal Pharma Ltd., Infectious Diseases / OTX and Consumer Healthcare.

Vifor is the latest pharmaceutical company to deploy Medgate’s OHS software which includes work safety software. Other pharmaceutical customers include Pfizer, GlaxoSmithKline, and Merck.

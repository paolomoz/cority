The Williams Companies Switches to Medgate

style
navy
  • Last Updated: December 13, 2011

Medgate is pleased to welcome Fortune 500 energy infrastructure company, The Williams Companies Inc., as a customer. Williams will be deploying Medgate’s Occupational Health and Safety software suites throughout its operations, and will also be replacing a competitor’s industrial hygiene data management product with Medgate’s Industrial Hygiene software suite.

Based out of Oklahoma, Williams is one of the leading energy infrastructure companies in North America. It owns interests in or operates 15,000 miles of interstate gas pipelines, 1,000 miles of NGL transportation pipelines, and more than 10,000 miles of oil and gas gathering pipelines. The company’s facilities have daily gas processing capacity of 6.6 billion cubic feet of natural gas and NGL production of more than 200,000 barrels per day.

Williams joins a number of energy companies using Medgate’s HSE software for OH&S data management, including BP, Total Petrochemicals, Marathon, Shell and Suncor.

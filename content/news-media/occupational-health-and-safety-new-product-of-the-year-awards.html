Cority Wins Multiple Occupational Health and Safety New Product of the Year Awards

Leading EHSQ SaaS provider’s Predictive Analytics and Ergonomics solutions recognized by esteemed panel of industry experts

Toronto, ON – October 16, 2018 – Cority, the most trusted environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) software for assuring client success, proudly announces it has won two New Product of the Year Awards from Occupational Health and Safety magazine. Cority received the highest scores in the EHS Software and Ergonomics categories for Cority Analytics and Ergonomics Management respectively.

Now in its tenth year, the Occupational Health and Safety New Product of the Year awards program honors the outstanding product development achievements of health and safety manufacturers whose products are considered particularly noteworthy in their ability to improve workplace safety. This year’s contest attracted entries in 26 award categories, with an independent panel of three highly qualified judges choosing the winners.

“The judges’ scores in our tenth annual New Product of the Year contest were extremely close, and all of the products entered this year earned very high marks from them. This was an outstanding showing by all of the entrants,” said Jerry Laws, Editor, Occupational Health and Safety. “Manufacturers in this industry are developing better products at a rapid clip, products that in many cases are connected, as well as products that are more comfortable and more protective for their customers’ workers. Informing our readers and online audience about the best new products available to solve their safety and health challenges has always been central to our magazine’s mission, and this contest is an essential part of our outreach to the industry.”

EHS Software Category Winner: Cority Analytics

Cority Analytics leverages the science of prediction to reduce workplace incidents. Using a robust predictive model, Cority Analytics enables EHS professionals to easily assess the quality of their data, benchmark performance, identify the likelihood of workplace incidents and take action to mitigate them.

Ergonomics Category Winner: Ergonomics Management

Cority’s comprehensive Ergonomics Management solution helps organizations improve ergonomic data collection and mitigate risk. Developed by Cority’s in-house experts, the solution helps ergonomists plan and build effective assessment programs, perform audits and inspections, and understand and prevent risks to keep employees healthy and productive across the enterprise.

“We’re proud to be recognized by Occupational Health and Safety in multiple categories this year,” said John Easton, VP Product Management, Cority. “Awards like this are a testament to our industry-leading true SaaS solutions that help organizations across all industries proactively manage and mitigate risk to keep their employees safe and healthy.”

To learn more about Cority’s industry-leading EHSQ solutions, visit www.cority.com.

myCority improves EHSQ outcomes while delivering a strong ROI

Toronto, Canada – February 6, 2018 – Cority, the most trusted environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) software for assuring client success, today announced the launch of myCority, the company’s next-generation mobile solution that enables immediate completion of critical EHSQ activities anytime, anywhere – regardless of device or location.

Built in consultation with Cority clients and certified EHSQ experts, myCority is the enhanced mobile-responsive extension of the company’s industry-leading SaaS solution. Designed to streamline data collection and standardize processes to enable quick and accurate task completion, users can login using any supported web browser to securely view, capture and submit information and drive real-time decision making.

“myCority provides an environment of limitless mobile EHSQ apps by extending the secure Cority Enterprise EHSQ platform to engage and empower every employee on any device,” said John Easton, Director of Product Management, Cority. “Our clients have told us they want the flexibility of a truly mobile solution to support their EHSQ initiatives without the hassle of managing multiple point solutions. We’ve responded by leading the industry with the most configurable, ubiquitous platform while providing a consistent user experience. myCority extends the capabilities and experience to every employee, increasing data collection in a user-friendly environment while decreasing the ongoing operational costs to the organization.”

Key capabilities of myCority include:

Intuitive user interface – simplifies the user experience for employees, enabling easier and more efficient completion of EHSQ activities

Enterprise-grade security on-the-go – encrypts all employee data between browser and server to ensure information is submitted securely, leveraging the best-in-class security capabilities of the Cority platform without keeping data on any device.

Real-time data capture – empowers employees to collect accurate data and complete assigned tasks including inspections, corrective actions, and questionnaires from any web browser.

Personalized mobile experiences – rapidly and easily configure and deploy role-specific solutions to employees.

Offline mode – enables employees without Internet access to complete events, audits, inspections, and surveys and submit when a connection is available.

Task status – provides EHS managers with insight into task status to ensure daily initiatives like audits and event reports are completed on time.

Responsive design – ensures a reliable, consistent user experience for all employees regardless of device.

To learn more about myCority, visit www.cority.com/ehsq-software/mobile-and-portal

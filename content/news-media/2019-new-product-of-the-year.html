Cority’s Air Emissions Management Solution Wins 2019 New Product of the Year Award from Environmental Protection

style
navy
  • Last Updated: November 5, 2025

Toronto, ON –June 20, 2019 – Cority, the most trusted provider of environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) software, today announced its Air Emissions Management solution has received Environmental Protection’s 2019 New Product of the Year Award in the Software/SaaS category.

Now in its 10th year, the Environmental Protection New Product of the Year Award honors the outstanding achievements of industry manufacturers whose products are considered particularly noteworthy for making environmental professionals’ jobs a little easier. This is the second consecutive year Cority has received a New Product of the Year award, previously winning multiple awards in 2018 for its Applicability Assessment and Waste Management solutions.

“Health and Safety/Cleanup, Software/SaaS, and New Technology – Industry were very competitive categories this year,” said Jerry Laws, Editor of Environmental Protection. “Our contest shows that environmental equipment manufacturers continue to do an outstanding job of meeting their customers’ needs in this fast-moving marketplace.”

From audits and inspections to air, water, and waste management, Cority offers the only unified true SaaS platform in the industry that enables organizations to streamline enterprise-wide EHSQ programs and processes and manage compliance requirements from a single, centralized solution. Cority’s Air Emissions Management software provides environmental professionals a comprehensive set of tools to make reporting to agencies on hazardous air pollutants, greenhouse gases, and ozone-depleting substances easier and to help ensure compliance with global regulations and legislation.

“It’s an honor to receive Environmental Protection’s New Product of the Year Award two years in a row,” said John Easton, VP Product Management, Cority. “This recognition speaks to our continuous innovation and we’re proud to consistently deliver enterprise-scale solutions that enable our clients to simplify environmental management, standardize air emissions calculations, and streamline regulatory reporting.”

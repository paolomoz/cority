Medgate at HSE Excellence Conference in February 2013

style
navy
  • Last Updated: January 31, 2013

As a leading provider of workplace health and safety software, Medgate Inc. is dedicated to developing and improving health, safety, and environmental (HSE) data management for the global HSE community via HSE compliance software. As part of this endeavour, Medgate will be participating as the sole exhibitor at ‘HSE Excellence: Journey to Goal Zero’, a conference taking place in Miami from February 6-8, 2013. Medgate is pleased to be sending its Director of Global Sales, Mike Jackson, to the conference.

Organized by Fleming Group, the HSE Excellence conference will be a premier gathering of HSE leaders from across the Americas. In alignment with conference themes around the use of technology to improve HSE management, Mike will be showcasing Medgate’s workplace safety software to attendees and discussing issues around improving HSE through electronic data management.

Conference delegates will be representing the oil and gas, mining and drilling, energy and utilities, and manufacturing industries. Since its inception in 1985, Medgate has been deployed within companies in these industries and today its customer list includes companies like BHP Billiton, Suncor Energy, Barrick Gold, and Talisman Energy. With over ten years’ experience at Medgate, Mike himself brings a wealth of experience in health and safety software implementations and can share insights into how customers are using Medgate’s safety software to improve processes.

Medgate’s workplace safety software suite is a powerful, intuitive, and flexible HSE software solution. Developed by Medgate’s own certified safety professionals with input from customers, the software supports a safety management software approach to safety and helps to manage incidents, mitigate risks, satisfy compliance requirements, identify problem areas, and track performance.

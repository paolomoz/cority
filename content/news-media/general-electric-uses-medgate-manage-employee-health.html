General Electric uses Medgate (now Cority) to Manage Employee Health

style
navy
  • Last Updated: November 17, 2025

Cority is pleased to announce that General Electric (GE) has upgraded to the latest version of the employee health management software. As a result, GE  will be able to leverage the advanced functionality in the Occupational Health suite to better manage its occupational health programs. Occupational Health nurses and doctors at GE have been using Medgate for years, to track and manage employee health data around the world.

In addition, GE will begin using Medgate Portal, which allows employees to view and track all their medical data in one secure location, and simplifies the scheduling of appointments with occupational health clinics, relieving nurses of this administrative burden.

With revenues of over $146bn, General Electric is one of the world’s biggest companies and most-recognizable brands. GE builds appliances, lighting, power systems and other products that help millions of homes, offices, factories and retail facilities work better.

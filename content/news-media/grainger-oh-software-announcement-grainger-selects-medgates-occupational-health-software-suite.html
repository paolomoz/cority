Grainger selects Medgate’s Occupational Health Software Suite

style
navy
  • Last Updated: March 25, 2012

Medgate is pleased to announce that its health software has been selected by Fortune 500 Company Grainger to manage its occupational health data. Grainger will deploy Medgate’s Occupational Health software suite using the Medgate hosted (SaaS) model from their international headquarters in Lake Forest, IL.

Grainger is a leading distributor of industrial supplies, MRO equipment, tools and materials. With access to over 900,000 industrial supply products available online and in their print catalog, they provide maintenance, repair, and operational solutions for 2 million businesses and institutions in 157 different countries.

Grainger was one of only 27 companies featured in both the Fortune 500 and Best Companies to work for lists in 2011 , and has over 18,500 employees.

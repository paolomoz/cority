Medgate’s Monica Melkonian to Lead Roundtable at AIHce 2011

style
navy
  • Last Updated: November 5, 2025

Monica Melkonian, a Medgate Industrial Hygiene expert and Senior Client Service Consultant has arranged a roundtable session at the 2011 American Industrial Hygiene Association Conference and Exposition (AIHce). The Roundtable, called “Using Computer Applications to Demonstrate the IH Business Case as Part of the Value Strategy,” will take place on Wednesday, May 18 from 1pm-3:30pm.

Industrial hygienists play a critical role within organizations to ensure the health and safety of workers and the general public. Many times it can be difficult to quantify for management the true value hygienists provide to an organization. Monica and her peers will present success stories and useful tips to help prove the value of industrial hygiene through computer applications.

Attendees can discover Medgate’s Industrial Hygiene and Safety software suites at the AIHce Expo, booth # 1435.

Monica wrote a related article for the December issue of The Synergist, a respected Industrial Hygiene magazine published by the American Industrial Hygiene Association (AIHA). The article, ‘Streamlined Management: How to Build a Business Case for OHS Databases,’ can be accessed by AIHA members.

The conference is taking place from May 14-19, 2011 in Portland, OR.

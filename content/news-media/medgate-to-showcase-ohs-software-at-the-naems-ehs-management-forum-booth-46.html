Medgate OH&S Software at the NAEM’s EHS Management Forum

Medgate is pleased to be exhibiting at the NAEM’s EHS Management Forum in Naples, Florida, which will take place at the Ritz-Carlton Resort on October 17 and 18, 2012. The EHS Management Forum is the premier event dedicated to showcasing best practices in EHS and sustainability management. With more than 500 EHS and sustainability managers, directors and vice presidents from leading companies, this is the largest annual gathering of EHS professionals.

Medgate Certified Industrial Hygienists and Registered Safety Professionals will be showcasing our industry-leading OHS software at booth #46. The Medgate software is an integrated, enterprise-wide solution which includes five core product suites to cater to the main functional areas in OH&S: Occupational Health, Safety, Industrial Hygiene, Environmental, and Ergonomics. Medgate’s advanced solution helps EHS professionals assess risks in the workplace and implement proactive measures that prevent all types of EHS incidents from happening in the first place.

In particular, the Medgate’s Environmental Software Suite helps companies streamline the tracking and collection of key corporate environmental data and satisfy Environmental Management Systems (EMS) requirements. Developed in collaboration with environmental professionals, this suite enables EHS professionals to centrally track all corporate environmental compliance requirements and to manage enterprise-wide environmental data in conjunction with other OH&S data for a “one system” approach to EHS/OHS software. In fact, “Compliance Management is a critical common thread connecting all of the elements of an SMS/EMS together, which work together to achieve the common goal of improved health, safety, and environmental performance” says John Easton, Medgate’s Product Manager, safety expert and author the article Keeping Pace with EHS Compliance Management.

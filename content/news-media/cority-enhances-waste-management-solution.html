Cority Enhances Waste Management Solution with Best-in-Class Functionality

Global EHS and Quality SaaS leader introduces market-leading capabilities to streamline the management of hazardous and regulated waste

Toronto, Canada, October 24, 2017 – Cority, the most trusted Environmental, Health, Safety and Quality (EHSQ) software for assuring client success, today announced the enhancement of its Waste Management solution, a significant expansion to the company’s industry-leading SaaS-based Environmental Management capabilities. With Cority’s new Waste Management solution, EHS professionals can streamline and standardize the tracking of waste generation, storage, and disposal across the enterprise and easily maintain global compliance.

Running a waste management program that enhances Zero Waste sustainability initiatives and complies with evolving regulations is a growing challenge for EHS professionals with operations spanning multiple geographies. Developed in consultation with industry professionals and waste management experts, Cority addresses these issues with a robust feature set that simplifies compliance management, centralizes all waste activity data for consistent reporting, and tracks waste streams from facility to container level.

“Our goal was to innovate the industry’s most advanced Waste Management solution to empower EHS professionals to markedly increase their operational efficiency by providing unprecedented visibility into the entire cradle-to-grave waste management process,” said John Easton, Director of Product Management, Cority. “Our clients will now have best-in-class capabilities to track and manage waste inventories across sites, ensure timely disposal of hazardous and universal waste, and eliminate potential environmental events.”

Key capabilities of Cority’s Waste Management solution include:

Waste Tracking streamlines the tracking of the entire waste lifecycle from generation and on-site storage to transport and disposal, ensuring waste is being moved off site in a timely manner

Reporting and Dashboards leverages Cority’s Business Intelligence tools to create reports that track the amount of waste generated across sites and notifies EHS professionals of approaching storage deadlines and waste threshold limits

Waste Labels and Manifesting enables employees to easily classify waste types and properties in accordance with relevant jurisdictions, and print essential waste labels from within the Cority platform that meet international standards

Container Level Tracking manages waste streams down to the container level to make tracking, billing and manifesting more efficient

Barcoding ensures quick and accurate tracking of individual containers as they move through the waste management process

Vendor Management ensures that only approved vendors are transporting and disposing of waste and makes it easy to associate shipments and invoice information with specific vendors

To learn more about Cority’s Waste Management solution, visit www.cority.com

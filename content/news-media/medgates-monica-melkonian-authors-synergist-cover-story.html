The April Issue of AIHA’s The Synergist features an article by Medgate’s Monica Melkonian, titled “Strategies for Selecting an IH Software Solution: How to Find the Best Fit for Your Organization”.

In the article, Monica provides detailed recommendations for organizations considering a software system to manage their industrial hygiene (IH) requirements. She examines all phases of the selection process, from assembling a project team to defining RFP requirements and dealing with shortlisted vendors. Monica’s insights come from years of experience as an IH software implementation consultant and product manager for Medgate, a global provider of occupational health and safety software. She is also a Certified Industrial Hygienist (CIH) and the current chair of the AIHA Computer Applications Committee.

Delegates to the 2013 American Industrial Hygiene conference and exhibition (AIHce) in Montreal will have the opportunity to hear Monica speak live about IH software. She will be giving a short presentation on Tuesday May 21 at 11:30am in the Expo Theatre.

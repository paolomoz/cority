Medgate is pleased to once again be exhibiting at the American Industrial Hygiene Conference and Exhibition (AIHce), which is taking place May 14-19 in Portland, OR.

Medgate professionals, including Certified Industrial Hygienists (CIHs), will be showcasing our industry-leading Industrial Hygiene software at booth #1435 in the Exposition Hall. Medgate’s Monica Melkonian is also leading a roundtable session on Wednesday offering Industrial Hygienists tools to help them promote the value of Industrial Hygiene within their organizations.

Come by and learn how your organization can benefit from Medgate’s IH software – the choice of world-class companies such as Barrick Gold, BHP Billiton, and many other Fortune and Global 500 companies.

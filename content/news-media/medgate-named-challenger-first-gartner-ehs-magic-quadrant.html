Medgate named Challenger in First Gartner EHS Magic Quadrant

style
navy
  • Last Updated: June 12, 2014

Medgate is delighted to have been recognized as a Challenger by Gartner Research in the first Magic Quadrant for Environmental, Health and Safety Management Software.

Gartner develop Magic Quadrants for various industries with the aim to provide a qualitative analysis into a market and its direction, maturity and participants. The new EHS Management Systems quadrant was created out of an observation that the environmental, health and safety management systems market is an increasingly global market that has seen significant growth and transformation in the past five years.

Factors focused on by the Gartner analysts included; Medgate’s growth, the high level of Medgate customer satisfaction, and Medgate’s increasing breadth of functionality, thanks to the release of Medgate’s Safety suite in the fall of 2011.

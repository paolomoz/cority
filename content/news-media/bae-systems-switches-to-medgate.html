BAE Systems Switches to Medgate

style
navy
  • Last Updated: March 10, 2011

Medgate is pleased to announce that BAE Systems will be implementing Medgate’s complete OHS software product to manage employee health and safety data across its US operations. The global defense and security company was previously using a competitor’s occupational health management software for data management.

BAE Systems delivers a full range of products and services for air, land and naval forces, as well as advanced electronics, security, information technology solutions and customer support services. In 2009 BAE Systems reported sales of £22.4 billion (US$ 36.2 billion). With customers in more than 100 countries, and approximately 100,000 employees BAE Systems is ranked at #238 on Fortune’s 2010 Global 500 list, and is the second largest global defense company (based on 2009 revenues).

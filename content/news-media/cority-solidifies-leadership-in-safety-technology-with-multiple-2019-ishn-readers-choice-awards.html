Cority Solidifies Leadership in Safety Technology with Multiple 2019 ISHN Readers’ Choice Awards

style
navy

Annual awards program recognizes the most innovative personal protection and occupational health and safety products.

Toronto, ON –July 11, 2019 – Cority, the most trusted provider of environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) software, today announced its Predictive Analytics and Process Hazard Analysis solutions have both received 2019 ISHN Readers’ Choice Awards for Safety Technology. This is Cority’s second consecutive year winning an ISHN Readers’ Choice Award, being recognized last year for its mobile solution, myCority.

Now in its 7th year, the ISHN Readers’ Choice Awards recognize the best in occupational health and safety products, with thousands of ISHN subscribers and industry professionals voting for their favorite safety equipment suppliers and technologies.

From audits and inspections to incident, risk, and compliance management, Cority offers the only unified true SaaS platform in the industry that enables organizations to streamline enterprise-wide health and safety processes and manage program requirements from a single, centralized solution.

Cority AnalyticsCority Analytics received the Readers’ Choice Award in the Data Collection and Analysis – Predictive Analytics category. Harnessing the science of prediction to reduce workplace incidents, Cority Analytics enables EHS professionals to leverage advanced modeling tools to delve deeper into their data to better understand why incidents occur and where to focus their efforts to reduce risk and improve program performance.

Process Hazard AnalysisCority’s Process Hazard Analysis solution received the Readers’ Choice Award in the EHS Management Software category. Cority’s Process Hazard Analysis enables EHS professionals to properly identify hazardous chemicals risks across their operations, drive continuous improvement by proactively mitigating these risks, and quickly demonstrate compliance with regulatory requirements.

“It’s an honor to be recognized by ISHN’s community of experts for the second year in a row,” said John Easton, VP Product Management, Cority. “Cority is built by EHS professionals for EHS professionals and being acknowledged by the industry leaders who rely on our solutions to reduce risk and improve safety culture is especially meaningful to us.”

To learn more about Cority’s Analytics and Process Hazard Analysis solutions, visit: https://www.cority.com/

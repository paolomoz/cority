Medgate has been named Tech Company of The Year

style
navy
  • Last Updated: June 16, 2016

Medgate Inc. has been named Tech Company of The Year at the Techconnex Technology Leadership Awards Gala 2016 in Toronto. The award recognizes business leadership, excellence in innovation and a commitment to the knowledge-based sector in the Greater Toronto Area (GTA).

To be considered for the Tech Company of The Year award an organization must demonstrate growing profitability, community involvement, thought leadership and sound business fundamentals. Organizations have operated a minimum of eight years, to allow judges to analyze the organization’s growth and track record.

Medgate has grown at over 25% per year for five consecutive years, emerging as a global leader in the field of Environmental, Health and Safety Software.

Medgate President Mark Wallace said: “Toronto is in the top tier of global technology hubs, so it is an honour to be recognized by our peers as Tech Company of the Year. Without the community around us, we would not have access to the rich pool of expertise and employees that brings us our success, so it’s vital that we play an active role.”

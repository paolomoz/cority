Cority Recognized as One of Canada’s Top Small and Medium Employers for Third Consecutive Year

Award recognizes the nation’s best workplaces with forward-thinking human resources policies

Toronto, ON – April 13, 2018 – Cority, the most trusted environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) software for assuring client success, today announced it has been named one of Canada’s Top Small & Medium Employers (SMEs) for the third year in a row by Mediacorp Canada Inc. This recognition comes on the heels of the company’s record worldwide growth. “This year, we’ve seen tremendous growth at many of the SME winners,” says Anthony Meehan, Publisher at Mediacorp. “It’s not uncommon to see year-over-year employment growth of 50% at some of the winners, providing tremendous advancement opportunities for their staff. It’s one of the reasons that small and medium employers have created more than 90% of the private-sector jobs in the past decade.”

Now in its 5th year, Canada’s Top Small & Medium Employers is an editorial competition that recognizes the small and medium enterprises (SMEs) with the nation’s best workplaces and forward-thinking human resources policies. Employers were evaluated by the editors at Canada’s Top 100 Employers eight criteria: Physical Workplace; Work Atmosphere and Social; Health, Financial and Family Benefits; Vacation and Time Off; Employee Communications; Performance Management; Training and Skills Development; and Community Involvement.

“It’s an honor to be recognized again as a top employer in Canada,” said Mark Wallace, President and CEO, Cority. “We continue to grow exponentially because of our exceptional culture and continued career development for our employees. This award is a testament to our team who embody our core values and are committed to helping our clients achieve superior environmental, health, safety, and quality results.” To learn more about Cority’s culture and current career opportunities, visit www.cority.com/careers.

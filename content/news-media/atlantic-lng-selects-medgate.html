Medgate is pleased to announce that Atlantic LNG is using the occupational health and industrial hygiene suites of its EHS software.

Atlantic LNG, based in Point Fortin, Trinidad, is one of the world’s largest producers of Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG).

Atlantic LNG takes natural gas from fields in and around Trinidad and Tobago to a four-train liquefaction facility, which is located in Point Fortin, on the southwest coast of Trinidad, where it is turned into LNG.

Medgate Welcomes Pam Bobbitt as Director of Channels & Product Marketing

style
navy
  • Last Updated: July 8, 2026

Medgate is pleased to welcome Pam Bobbitt as the new Director of Channels & Product Marketing. Pam is in charge of expanding Medgate’s partner program, and will leverage her deep expertise in both EHS processes and the software industry to provide best practices which will ensure the success of her endeavours.

Having trained as a chemist, Pam has spent more than 15 years as an EHS professional in the pharmaceutical, chemical and automotive industries. Most recently, she spent four years working at Enablon in various roles, ultimately being appointed to Director of Sales for Europe, the Middle East and Africa. At Medgate, she will use her EHS industry expertise to translate business requirements into successful software programs.

“We’re thrilled to have Pam onboard, and we’re looking forward to seeing the partner program grow and flourish,” said Medgate President Mark Wallace. “Pam’s connections and deep understanding of the EHS industry will be a crucial part of Medgate’s continued growth.” “When I heard the number and depth of experience of EHS professionals already working at Medgate, I was sold.” Pam said, “Nothing can replace that when it comes to speaking the same language as our customers to build lasting, beneficial partnerships. Successful programs must have the right combination of domain expertise to map business into the technology. I am excited to create that these programs for the Medgate community with key partners.”

Pam will be speaking as an EHS subject matter expert next week at the Verdantix Summit 2016 in Amsterdam, The Netherlands.

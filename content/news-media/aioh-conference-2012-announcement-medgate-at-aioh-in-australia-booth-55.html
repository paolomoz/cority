Medgate at AIOH in Australia

Medgate Inc., the leading global provider of software for OH&S data management, will be showcasing its Occupational Hygiene Software at the Australian Institute of Occupational Hygienists (AIOH) conference in Adelaide, South Australia.

Hot on the heels of the opening of its Australian office, Medgate will be presenting its occupational hygiene software at the Australian Institute of Occupational Hygienists conference in Adelaide, South Australia, from December 2 to 4, 2012. Tanya Bose, General Manager of Medgate Australia, will be showcasing the OH&S software company’s offerings at booth #55 in the AIOH exposition hall.

Over 500 occupational hygiene professionals are expected to attend the conference at the Adelaide Convention Centre. Occupational hygiene researchers, practitioners and regulators across various industries will meet to discuss issues around the conference theme of ‘meeting global challenges in occupational hygiene’.

Medgate’s comprehensive software is in use within various organisations around the globe, including a number of large mining companies with key operations in Australia. At the AIOH conference, Medgate looks forward to engaging participants with product demonstrations and sharing the benefits its customers are realizing. These benefits are clearly articulated by a Senior Manager at Barrick Gold who states that “Medgate has allowed us to collect and manage Industrial Hygiene data more efficiently and consistently than previous methods, which has enhanced our ability to focus on specific issues and improve the working environment for our employees.” (read the Medgate -Barrick Gold Case Study).

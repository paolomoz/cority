Medgate Kicks Off Middle-East Roadshow

style
navy
  • Last Updated: September 18, 2012

Global leader in Occupational Health and Safety software, Medgate, to showcase its integrated OHS software platform at 8th HSE Forum in Energy in Doha, Qatar, and at open ‘Meet and Greet’ event in Dubai, UAE.

This will be the company’s first Middle-East roadshow series from October 7 to 10, 2012.

8th HSE Forum in Energy in Doha, Qatar – Booth #A04

Medgate is pleased to be exhibiting at the 8th HSE Forum in Energy at the Grand Hyatt Hotel in Doha, Qatar, on October 8 and 9. At this conference, safety and health experts form the energy sector will be looking for strategies to reduce workplace incidents and related costs. Developed by certified safety professionals and featured at the Forum will be Medgate’s enterprise-wide safety suite which helps professionals manage incidents, pro-actively mitigate risks, satisfy compliance requirements, identify problem areas, and track performance.

Medgate ‘Meet and Greet’ in Dubai, UAE Mike Jackson, Global Sales Director, and Jo Henderson, EMEA Sales Manager, invite OH&S professionals to attend an informal information session on the 10th of October at the Fairmont Hotel in Dubai. This will be the opportunity to demonstrate how Medgate can help make informed decisions on how to improve employee health and productivity using its HSE compliance software, medical clinic software, safety inspection software, etc, and discuss health and safety needs. OHS professionals can register for one of the two sessions on the event website.

Medgate has an international presence, with client sites in the North America, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, South America, and Australia. Use of the Medgate platform in the Middle East has been growing over the past several years as international firms increase their presence in the market. The roadshow series provides an opportunity for firms to meet with the Medgate team and see the OHS platform in action. “We are looking forward to this roadshow series in Doha and Dubai where we will be able to talk more about the significant advantages and benefits to be gained from using our OHS software platform, which is considered to be the best in the world”, said Mike Jackson, Global Sales Director.

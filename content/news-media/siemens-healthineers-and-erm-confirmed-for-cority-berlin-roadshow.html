Siemens Healthineers, ERM and RegScan confirmed for Cority Berlin Roadshow

Birmingham, UK – 11th October 2018

Leading Environmental, Health, Safety and Quality (EHSQ) software provider, Cority will be attending the EHS International Congress in Berlin, Germany from 7-8 November 2018. The exhibition will follow the Cority German Roadshow on 6 November at the Radisson Blu, Berlin. 

Cority Roadshow – Berlin

Gone are the days of silos and point solutions. Today, as organizations focus on higher-level priorities like Sustainability, Operational Excellence, Worker Wellbeing and Digital Transformation whilst adapting to Industry 4.0, ISO 45001 and more, the role of the SaaS-based EHSQ software becomes even more important.

On Tuesday 6th November we will be hosting our one-day Cority Roadshow in Berlin. During this event, global provider of environmental, health, safety and risk consulting services, ERM, will offer practical advice to help you manage digital compliance and RegScan will highlight recent key regulatory changes in Germany and its associated impacts on business.

Siemens Healthineers will also speak about its journey to achieve 4 main objectives: Serve the Environment, Product Eco Excellence, Health Management, Zero Harm Culture and the challenges and lessons learned to drive the program to better business outcomes.

EHS International Congress

The EHS International Congress series is Europe’s health & safety platform that allows EHS experts from the world’s largest companies (GSK, L’Oréal and Siemens to name a few) to discuss current projects through which they can improve their decision making and leverage the new processes for successful initiatives.

During the two-day event, Cority will be demonstrating its award-winning SaaS-based EHSQ platform that is already helping organizations all over the world to improve workplace health and safety initiatives, proactively mitigate risk, and streamline complex EHSQ processes across the enterprise to achieve operational excellence.

Connect with a member of the Cority team at the EHS International Congress register or visit our homepage to schedule a personalised demo.

Cority Wins National Award for Business Excellence

style
navy
  • Last Updated: September 6, 2018

Toronto, ON, – September 6, 2018 – Cority, the most trusted environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) software for assuring client success, has been named a winner of the Canadian Business Excellence Award for Private Businesses for the second year.

The national award recognizes outstanding achievements by organizations in the private sector that clearly demonstrate a strategic approach to successfully improving business performance and achieving goals. All applicants were valuated by the Awards Adjudication Committee, a prestigious panel of business experts from organizations including, BC Business Magazine, CEO Global Network, and Carleton University, who looked for organizations that out-perform the competition in three key performance areas: Delighted Customers, Engaged Employees, and Innovation.

“I am very proud that Cority has once again been recognized for its accomplishments,” says CEO Mark Wallace. “The exceptional culture we have cultivated has fueled our rapid growth and innovation. As we continue to expand globally, it’s critical to keep our core values in sight, as they are the pillars that foster continuously high rates of satisfaction among our customers and employees alike.”

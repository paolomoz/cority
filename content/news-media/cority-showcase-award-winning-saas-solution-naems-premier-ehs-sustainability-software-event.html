Cority to Showcase Award-Winning SaaS Solution at NAEM’S Premier EHS and Sustainability Software Event

Cority client, Candice Fletcher from Merck, to lead session on EHS data quality best practices

Toronto, Canada – March 5, 2018 – Cority, the most trusted environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) software for assuring client success, is pleased to announce it will be attending NAEM’s EHS and Sustainability Software and Data Management Conference from March 5-7, with one of its clients leading a breakout session on leveraging data to improve EHS performance.

Candice Fletcher, Operations Specialist at Merck, will be presenting How Merck Increased the Accuracy of their Data Collection on Wednesday, March 7 from 11:15 am – 12:15 pm. During her session, Candice will discuss how Merck improved data quality and reporting by streamlining procedures and documentation across sites using Cority’s solution.

Cority is a Terabyte sponsor and will showcase its industry-leading EHSQ SaaS platform at booth #103. Designed by certified industry experts, Cority’s solution empowers organizations to centralize, streamline and standardize critical EHS and Quality information and risk management across the enterprise.

To see Cority’s environmental, health and safety software solution in action, stop by booth #103 or visit www.cority.com to schedule a personalized demo.

About Cority

Cority gives every employee from the field to the board room the power to make a difference, reducing risks and creating a safer, healthier, and more sustainable world. For over 35 years, Cority’s people-first software solutions have been built by EHS and sustainability experts who know the pressures businesses face. Time-tested, scalable, and configurable, CorityOne is the responsible business platform that combines datasets from across the organization to enable improved efficiencies, actionable insights, data-driven decisions, and more accurate reporting on performance. Trusted by over 1400 organizations worldwide, Cority deeply cares about helping people work toward a better future for everyone. To learn more, visit www.cority.com.

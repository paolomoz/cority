Cority Enhances Environmental Management Solution with Comprehensive Air Emissions Inventory

New functionality standardizes air emissions data collection and calculations and streamlines regulatory reporting across the enterprise

Toronto, Canada – July 17, 2018 – Cority, the most trusted environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) software for assuring client success, today introduced a new Air Emissions Inventory module into its industry-leading Environmental Management solution.

Developed by experts with decades of combined experience across various sectors, including oil and gas, chemical manufacturing, and electric utilities, Cority’s Air Emissions Inventory module provides clients with a centralized repository to collect, calculate, review, and report asset level air emissions data to regulators.

“A single facility can have dozens of emission points that need to be monitored,” said John Easton, Director of Product Management, Cority. “For most companies, air emissions management is a decentralized process – which means it happens at the facility, not at corporate. To help our clients centralize and streamline how they manage their regulatory compliance programs, we’ve made it easy to collect data, run calculations, identify errors, and comply with applicable regulations and emission limits.”

Key capabilities of Cority’s Air Emissions Inventory include:

Robust Calculation Engine – performs complex calculations on large datasets to generate source profiles for hazardous air pollutants

Global Solution – works across industry sectors and geographies

Streamline Processes – uses out of the box emission factors and equations or allows you to add your own to ensure facilities are calculating their emissions properly

Centralized Repository – consolidates data from disparate systems and spreadsheets into a single location, providing complete visibility into all locations and sites

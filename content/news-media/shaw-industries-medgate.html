Shaw Industries selects Medgate

Medgate is pleased to announce that Shaw Industries is the latest company to implement its Environmental, Health and Safety (EHS) Data Management Software.

Headquartered in Dalton, Gerogia, Shaw Industries is the world’s largest carpet manufacturer and a leading flooring provider. With revenues of more than $4.8bn (USD) and employing over 22,000 people globally, Shaw is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway.

Shaw has implemented Medgate’s Occupational Health suite, which provides specialized tools to manage the health information of its employees.

Rolls-Royce Selects Medgate EHS Software

style
navy
  • Last Updated: April 16, 2014

April 18, 2014 – Medgate is pleased to welcome engine maker Rolls-Royce as a new customer. Utilizing Medgate’s Occupational Health suite, and hosted on the Microsoft Azure platform, the Rolls-Royce project successfully demonstrated that European medical data can be accessed, converted and hosted on the Medgate platform without ever needing to leave Europe.

Medgate’s commitment to data security and privacy is paramount. The first EHS software solutions platform to achieve ISO 27001:2013 certification, Medgate adheres to the Safe Harbor principles, as well as other stringent data security practices worldwide. To read more about data security and privacy in EHS software, please read our Fact Sheet.

More about Rolls Royce

Rolls-Royce are a global provider of integrated power systems and services to the civil aerospace, defense aerospace, marine and energy markets. They have customers in more than 120 countries, comprising more than 380 airlines and leasing customers, 160 armed forces, 4,000 marine customers, including 70 navies, and 1,600 energy and nuclear customers. In 2013, Rolls-Royce invested £1.1 billion on research and development. They also support a global network of 31 University Technology Centers, which position Rolls-Royce engineers at the forefront of scientific research.

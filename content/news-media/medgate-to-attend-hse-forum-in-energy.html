Medgate to Attend HSE Forum in Energy

style
navy
  • Last Updated: September 26, 2013

Medgate is pleased to announce its participation in the 9th Annual HSE Forum in Energy, taking place in Doha, Qatar from September 30 to October 2, 2013. The company will showcase its leading occupational health and safety (OHS) software products and consult with the region’s OHS professionals on implementing effective OHS business processes. Delegates can find Medgate at booth D6.

Medgate’s appearance at the Middle East’s premier Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) conference reflects the company’s commitment to OHS advancements in the EMEA region. With satellite offices in the U.K. and Australia, the company is well positioned to support the requirements of its EMEA customer base, which includes some of the region’s most notable organizations.

“We are looking forward to meeting with HSE delegates to discuss strategies in OHS data management,” states Mike Jackson, Director of Global Sales at Medgate. “Organizations in the Middle East are informed and eager to implement processes that promote OHS improvements. The Medgate HSE software is a highly configurable solution that can not only adapt to unique regional requirements but can help an organization fulfill its OHS performance goals.”

OH&S professionals representing the oil and gas, mining and drilling, energy and utilities, and manufacturing industries will participate in the conference. Since its inception in 1985, Medgate has been deployed within companies in these industries and today its customer list includes companies like BHP Billiton, Suncor Energy, Barrick Gold, and Talisman Energy.

The HSE Forum in Energy is the annual platform where HSE professionals, government and regulatory bodies, oil and gas companies, HSE advocates, academics and medical experts come to meet in support to reduce workplace fatalities, maintain employee well-being and ensure sustainable environmental protection. Officially supported by Qatar Petroleum and endorsed by ASSE, IPIECA, RoSPA, BOHS, IIRSM and British Safety Council, the forum with co-located exhibition will be held under the patronage of H.E. Dr. Mohammed Bin Saleh Al-Sada, Minister of Energy & Industry and Chairman of Qatar Petroleum.

Medgate’s workplace safety software suite is a powerful, intuitive, and flexible software solution. Developed by Medgate’s own certified safety professionals with input from customers, the software supports a safety management system approach to safety and helps to manage incidents, mitigate risks, satisfy compliance requirements, identify problem areas, and track performance.

Cority Recognized as One of Canada’s Top Technology Companies

Annual Branham300 listing recognizes achievements of innovative Canadian technology firms

Toronto, Canada – June 27, 2018 – Cority, the most trusted environmental, health, safety, and quality (EHSQ) software provider, today announced it has been recognized by the Branham Group as one of the top 250 Information and Communication Technology (ICT) companies in Canada.

Now in its 25th year, the Branham300 is the best-known and most widely referenced listing of Canada’s top public and private ICT companies, ranked by revenue. This year, Cority moved up the ranking to number 98.

“It’s an honor to be included on the Braham300 list again,” said Mark Wallace, President and CEO, Cority. “Rising up the ranking this year is a testament to the success of our clients, our culture, and our industry-leading EHSQ SaaS solution, which continue to fuel our unprecedented growth and global expansion.”

“The 2018 edition of the Branham300 once again shows revenue growth in Canada’s ICT industry. Revenue generated by the Top 250 tech companies in Canada has grown every year since 2009. Canada’s ICT professionals can be very proud of both the growth and the innovation they produced last year, and we are once again pleased to shine a congratulatory spotlight on this critical industry,” said Wayne Gudbranson, CEO, Branham Group.

The announcement comes on the heels of Cority’s recent recognition for product innovation, having received the 2018 ISHN Readers’ Choice Award for its mobile-first solution, myCority, and two Environmental Protection New Product of the Year Awards for its Waste Management and Applicability solutions.

Medgate to Showcase Occupational Health Software at AAOHN & AOHC

style
navy
  • Last Updated: March 30, 2012

Medgate Inc., the leading global provider of software for health and safety data management, will be showcasing its Occupational Health Software solution at this year’s American Association of Occupational Health Nurses (AAOHN) in Nashville, Tennessee and at the American Occupational Health Conference (AOHC) in Los Angeles, California.

AAOHN – Medgate Booth 411

The AAOHN Conference will take place from April 22 to 25 at the Gaylord Opryland Resort & Convention Center located in Nashville, Tennessee. Medgate is looking forward to once again showcasing its software to more than 900 occupational health professionals specifically the newest release of the platform, Medgate GX2. The AAOHN Annual Conference aims to explore the latest technology and resources to improve attendees’ management of occupational and environmental health and safety practice, and enhance their skills in case management and health promotion and risk reduction. Medgate will have the opportunity to show how its software capabilities can help occupational health professionals streamline medical surveillance, standardize employee medical records, and manage clinic visits.

AOHC – Medgate Booth 126

The AOHC Conference will take place from April 29 to May 2 at the Hyatt Regency Century Plaza in Los Angeles, California. The AOHC is a premier event that fosters education and progression in occupational medicine, and Medgate will be showcasing our cutting edge Occupational Health software solution again this year. More than 1,000 physicians and other senior occupational health professionals are expected to attend the conference and learn about the latest products and services. “These individuals are charged with the important responsibility of managing and improving employee health and productivity and require intuitive, comprehensive solutions to support and enhance their efforts” states Mark Wallace, President of Medgate, who will personally attend to represent the company. “As the leaders in our field, we have been responding and adapting to the unique needs of Occupational Health professionals for over 25 years. At this year’s AOHC, we are looking forward to the always invaluable knowledge exchange between old friends and new faces.”

Medgate’s Occupational Health and Safety software solutions are used by many of the AAOHN and AOHC members, including occupational health physicians for government agencies and the Fortune 500 group of companies. The software is used to manage all aspects of occupational medicine, including comprehensive medical surveillance, scheduling, clinical testing, and electronic medical records.

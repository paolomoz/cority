Kuwait Oil Company improves health and safety with Medgate

style
navy
  • Last Updated: March 25, 2015

We are delighted to announce that Kuwait Oil Company (KOC) has chosen to use Medgate’s Industrial Hygiene suite to help create a healthier working environment for its employees. KOC is Medgate’s first client in Kuwait adds to our growing client list in the GCC. Medgate worked with Integrated Environmental Solutions, a global environmental technology consultancy, to carry out this project.

Founded in 1934, KOC is the world’s fourth-largest oil exporter. It is a subsidiary of a Government-owned holding company, Kuwait Petroleum Corporation. At full capacity, KOC produces almost 3 million barrels of crude oil per day.

KOC has won several awards for its occupational health and safety programs and the implementation of Medgate reaffirms its commitment to providing a safe and healthy working environment for its employees.

Southern Company using Medgate to help keep employees safe

style

Medgate is delighted to announce that Southern Company has recently gone live on the Medgate platform to manage its environmental, health and safety data processes.

Based out of Atlanta, Southern Company is a collection of power companies in the south-eastern United States, including Alabama Power, Georgia Power, Mississippi Power, Southern Nuclear, and a few others. A Fortune 500 company, with a market value of over $25bn, Southern Company ranks among the twenty largest utility companies in the world.

Southern Company is using the Safety and Industrial Hygiene suites in Medgate. It joins a growing list of utility companies that use Medgate for EHS data management.

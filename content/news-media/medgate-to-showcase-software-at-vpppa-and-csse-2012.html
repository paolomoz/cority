Medgate to Showcase Software at VPPPA and CSSE 2012

  • Last Updated: August 16, 2012

Medgate is pleased to be exhibiting their OHS management software at the VPPPA Conference show in Anaheim, and the CSSE show in Niagara Falls. The Medgate OHS Management Software is an integrated, enterprise-wide medical case management software solution. It helps professionals assess risks in the workplace and implement proactive measures that prevent all types of incidents – illnesses, injuries, and other and other EHS incidents – from happening in the first place.

VPPPA – Medgate Booth 349

The 28th Annual National VPPPA Conference will take place August 20-23, 2012, at the Anaheim Convention Center in Anaheim, CA. This event is one of the premier safety and health conferences in the United States featuring best practice sharing and professional networking in Voluntary Protection Programs (VPP) and the environmental and occupational safety and health industry. Medgate is an affiliate member of the Voluntary Protection Programs Participants’ Association (VPPPA). The “Voluntary Protection Program” (VPP) is an OSHA initiative in where OSHA, management, and labor work cooperatively to prevent occupational fatalities, injuries, and illnesses through a system focused on: hazard prevention and control; worksite analysis; training; and management commitment and worker involvement. More than fifty VPP sites use Medgate HSE software solutions to manage their health and safety programs.

CSSE – Medgate Booth 155

The Canadian Society of Safety Engineering (CSSE) Annual Conference will be held in Niagara Falls, Ontario, from September 9 to 12. Attendees will have to opportunity to learn from OH&S subject matter experts on successful health and safety tools and programs. Alongside the conference, the 43rd CSSE HSE Exhibition will take place at the Sheraton on the Falls Hotel.

At these shows, Medgate will be showcasing its industry-leading Health and Safety Software. The Medgate solution includes five core product suites to cater to the main functional areas in OH&S: Occupational Health, Safety, Industrial Hygiene, Environmental, and Ergonomics. Medgate’s advanced solution helps EHS professionals improve employee productivity, and these shows will be opportunities to demonstrate how the Medgate solution can help them:

  • track medical trends, manage compliance and regulatory requirements, and mitigate employee absences
  • satisfy safety compliance requirements, identify problem areas, manage incidents and pro-actively mitigate risks
  • centralize and streamline the tracking and collection of key corporate environmental data and satisfy Environmental Management Systems (EMS) requirements

Medgate Expands Global Sales and Support

Medgate Inc., the leading global provider of occupational health and safety software, is extending its reach in the international occupational health and safety (OH&S) software market by expanding its Australian-based operations.

Perth, Australia – Global health and safety software company Medgate Inc. is pleased to announce that it has opened an office in Australia. Continuing its growth in markets around the world, the new Medgate office will provide sales and product support to organisations in Australia and Asia.

Tanya Bose, recently named General Manager of Medgate Australia PTY Ltd. will lead the new operation. Tanya has over seven years of experience in the occupational health and environmental safety field, is a Certified Registered Safety Professional, and has worked as a Senior Client Service Consultant for Medgate for clients from a wide range of industries.

Medgate’s established global presence includes a client roster of Global 500 companies and multi- national government bodies. Around the globe, occupational health and safety requirements are becoming increasingly stringent and expansive, which has strengthened the need for organisations to implement integrated, enterprise-wide OH&S software systems. These systems focus on helping companies to improve worker health and productivity and manage compliance with federal, local, and corporate OH&S regulations. Medgate’s software offers robust globalisation features that allow large organisations to standardize processes across multiple sites and countries. The company is continuously evolving its software offering to respond to and anticipate the requirements of its international clientele. “Medgate has secured a strong presence in the international marketplace,” states Mark Wallace, President of Medgate. “Establishing an office in Australia means that we can further accelerate this momentum and provide additional sales and consulting support to the global OH&S community”.

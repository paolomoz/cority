Medgate’s President Honoured with AceTech Ontario’s Leadership Award

style
navy
  • Last Updated: July 8, 2026

AceTech Ontario, an organization that provides education, networking, performance and leadership programs presented Mark Wallace and four other CEOs with awards for success, innovation and leadership at its 2012 Annual Retreat.

Mark Wallace was singled-out for his leadership in growing Medgate in recent years into a leading global company. Medgate, the global leader in OH&S software, is available in multiple languages and used across the globe in hundreds of the best known corporations and government entities.

“It is very gratifying to receive this award from a network of peers that I both respect and admire,” Mark says. “I have learned a lot through my participation with AceTech Ontario; it is a great honour to be recognized in this way.”

Composed exclusively of CEOs of technology companies, AceTech Ontario’s mission is to build an environment that promotes continuous improvement across all business activities. It provides members opportunities for rich discussion, mentoring, peer guidance, and strategy development, as well as the exchange of real-world experiences.

AceTech’s 2012 Annual Retreat, held in the beautiful Blue Mountains north of Toronto, was a three-day event featuring talks on strategy, sales, growth and more. Mark’s award was presented by Mark Miller, CEO of Volaris Group of software companies, and the leader of Mark’s AceTech roundtable. “Mark has done a terrific job of growing Medgate, including a recent acquisition of a UK competitor. On a personal note, he listens and he is truly interested in making Medgate and its employees the best they can be. As such, he is a natural for AceTech which is built upon collaboration, sharing of best practices, and continuous improvement.”

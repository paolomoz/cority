Worcestershire County Council Deploy Medgate GX2

style
navy
  • Last Updated: August 13, 2014

Medgate is pleased to announce Worcestershire County Council as the latest client to go live on GX2. They will be using the Incident Management module to track injuries for council employees, as well as school and academy students across the county.

Worcestershire County Council’s data will be stored on Medgate’s Microsoft Azure cloud-hosting platform.

Medgate’s Incident Management module is part of the GX2 Safety suite, developed by qualified safety professionals to help organizations meet international compliance standards.

More about Worcestershire County Council

Worcestershire County Council governs the county of Worcestershire in England, covering an area of 672 square miles, with 566,500 residents. The County Council manages an annual budget of $1.4bn.  Almost 77,000 students are enrolled in Worcestershire’s schools and academies.

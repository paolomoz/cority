Vantaz, Medgate’s partner in Latin America, will be hosting an event on April 23 in Santiago, Chile. The event, Cultural Change in Occupational Health and Safety: The Key to Success, will take place from at 9am on April 23.

Medgate is the leading global provider of software for environmental, health, and safety data management. Vantaz is an international management consulting firm dedicated to transformation in mining.

In 2013, the two companies signed an agreement under which Vantaz serves as a Medgate representative in Chile and regions of South America, providing sales support and consulting services to customers implementing the Medgate EHS software.

International environmental, health and safety experts will speak at the event, including Mike Jackson, Medgate’s Senior Director of Global Sales, Mauro Mezzano, CEO of Vantaz, and Hans Sandoval, Health and Safety Manager at the Doña Inés de Collahuasi Mining Company.

Decision makers from mining companies will be present to share their experiences and opinions.

“The Medgate-Vantaz partnership is founded on common expertise between our two companies. With a demonstrated knowledge of risk, safety, and environmental best practices, Vantaz is well-positioned to bring Medgate’s solutions to Chile and support the complex needs of our customers,” says Mike Jackson.

“Medgate and Vantaz both have an established presence in the natural resources sector. Mining giants BHP Billiton and Barrick Gold are already working with both companies, while the Medgate customer list also includes Newmont, Rio Tinto, and several of the world’s largest oil & gas companies.”

Medgate Australia GM Achieves CMPSIA Certification

style
navy
  • Last Updated: October 1, 2013

Medgate, the leading provider of enterprise EH&S software, is pleased to announce that Tanya Bose, General Manager of Medgate Australia, has achieved Chartered Professional Member status of the Safety Institute of Australia (CMPSIA). The certification is granted to Australian EH&S professionals who demonstrate fulfillment of a strict set of professional requirements.

“Medgate wholeheartedly congratulates Tanya on this achievement,” states Jill Brown, VP of Client Service Consulting at Medgate. “Medgate not only provides software, but partners with our customers to implement successful EH&S processes. Tanya’s certification is a testament to our team’s expertise and exemplary of her commitment to the Australian OH&S industry.”

As Medgate’s General Manager, Australia, Tanya is responsible for sales, services, and support for Australian organizations implementing Medgate’s Environmental Health & Safety software. Services include business process reviews, configuration, and training for Medgate software implementations. In addition to the CMPSIA and RSP certification, Tanya is also a Canadian Registered Safety Professional (CRSP) and holds a Diploma in Occupational Health and Safety (DOHS).

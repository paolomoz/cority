Cority Expands International Footprint

style
navy
  • Last Updated: August 7, 2018

Cority, global leader in enterprise-grade, SaaS-based Environmental, Health, Safety, and Quality (EHS) risk management solutions continues to expand in international markets as it appoints new European business development and marketing team, showing a continued push to take the global EHS market by storm.

Based in the newly acquired office space in central Birmingham, UK, the new team sit alongside Cority subsidiary Cohort bringing the total number of European employees to 77. The business and team in Europe have grown immensely over the last few years, necessitating the need to double UK office space. Being in the center of Birmingham, Cority are perfectly positioned to make the most of Birmingham’s connectivity and international status as an important automotive, manufacturing and medical hub.

“Our presence in Europe is well-established, where we have key clients including BP, Jaguar Land Rover, GlaxoSmithKline, Ford UK, and United Nations but there’s room for more.” said Mike Jackson, Senior Director of International Sales. “With our growing European team, we are now even more accessible to our local customer base to provide them with the support they need to maximise the value of their Cority investment whilst also allowing us to better respond to regional business demands.”

In addition to expanding the sales and marketing team in Europe, Cority is making further traction in France by scoring highly in French analyst firm, Cabinet de St. Front 2018 Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Benchmark Report whilst also adding aerospace giant, Airbus to its list of enterprise clients, forming the foundation of its new FISH (Federated Information for Safety and Health) platform.

Critical to its success in expanding market share and growing relationships with customers is the Cority Roadshow series for EHS professionals looking to explore and build best practices with Cority consultants and industry leaders. In addition to its North America events, this year Cority have added Paris, Berlin, London and Melbourne to its Roadshow line-up. Jackson commented “For our international Roadshows, we are introducing new dynamic and localized content, tailoring each event to each region’s emerging issues, making them even more worthwhile and accessible.” For more information on Cority Roadshows visit www.cority.com/events

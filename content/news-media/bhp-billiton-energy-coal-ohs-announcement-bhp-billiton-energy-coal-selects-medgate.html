BHP Billiton Energy Coal Selects Medgate

style
navy
  • Last Updated: February 3, 2011

BHP Billiton Energy Coal is the latest BHP Billiton business to select Medgate. Like the other BHP businesses, the Energy Coal division will be implementing Medgate’s software to manage Occupational Health and Occupational Hygiene across its global operations, which includes deployments in Australia, North America, South America, South Africa, and Indonesia.

BHP Billiton is one of the world’s largest producers and marketers of export thermal coal. BHP Billiton provides thermal coal for use in the electric power generation industry and general industrial concerns such as cement production.

Medgate is pleased to be continuing a successful partnership with this Australian-based global organization. With about 50,000 employees, BHP Billiton ranks 139th on the Global 500 listing of the world’s largest companies.

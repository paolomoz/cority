Understand the EHS Software Trends Driving Digital Transformation in 2025

The Verdantix Global Corporate Survey 2025: EHS Budgets, Priorities and Tech Preferences delivers a data-backed view into the EHS software trends and technology investments that will define the year ahead. Drawing insights from global EHS, sustainability, and operations leaders, the report highlights how organizations are increasing budgets to modernize systems, improve compliance, and enable smarter, data-driven decisions.

Findings show strong momentum toward AI in EHS, connected platforms, and advanced analytics — with spending focused on solutions that integrate environmental, health, safety, and sustainability data across the enterprise. Companies are also investing in contractor management software and tools that simplify environmental compliance and performance reporting.

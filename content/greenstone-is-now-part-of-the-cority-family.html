Greenstone: Data that talks to you

On May 4, 2023 Cority announced its acquisition of Greenstone, a UK-headquartered software company with a suite of sustainability solutions for enterprises, supply chains, and asset managers. Through this acquisition, the global enterprise EHS provider was able to complement it’s market-leading CorityOne solution with a suite of award-winning sustainability reporting software solutions that enable private equity and corporate organizations to achieve their sustainability goals and empower responsible businesses.

Founded in 2006, Greenstone has built long-term partnerships with its clients and constantly developed innovative solutions to stay ahead of their evolving sustainability management and ESG reporting requirements. Since then, Greenstone has provided Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solutions to clients in over 100 countries and across 15 industry sectors.

The Greenstone promise to clients is the delivery of ‘data that talks to you’ which focuses on ensuring its clients’ data delivers meaningful information in an easy-to-understand format that they can derive actionable insights from. In line with this philosophy, Greenstone enables and supports their customers by developing sustainability reporting software that supports their users no matter where they are in their sustainability journeys.
